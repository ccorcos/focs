           STAFF REPORT

Meeting Date:       September 17, 2025
To:                 Board of Directors
From:               Michael J. Aho, District Administrator
Subject:            Recognition of the Fair Oaks Theatre Festival for
                    Outstanding Service to the Community


 I.   Recommendation
      Staff recommends the Board of Directors adopt the attached Resolution
      recognizing the Fair Oaks Theatre Festival (FOTF) for over four decades of
      dedicated service to the community of Fair Oaks.

II.   Background
      The Fair Oaks Theatre Festival was founded more than 40 years ago by a
      dedicated group of community members and business leaders who
      envisioned the revitalization of the underutilized Veterans Memorial
      Amphitheater. Under the founding leadership of Wendell and Lois Frazier,
      and later by committed leaders such as Tony and Barbara Sestito, FOTF
      developed into a cherished cultural institution within the Sacramento
      region.

      Over the decades, the Festival became known for high-quality
      Shakespearean productions, community theater performances,
      educational programs, and the mentorship of emerging artists. The
      dedication of countless volunteers, actors, directors, technicians, and
      supporters made it possible for the Festival to enrich the lives of
      thousands of residents and visitors.

      Community partnerships with organizations such as American River
      College, Fair Oaks Rotary Club, Fair Oaks Chamber of Commerce, Fair Oaks
      Brew Pub, and the Fair Oaks Recreation and Park District played a key role
      in the Festival’s long-term success.
III.   Problem /Situation/ Request
       The Fair Oaks Theatre Festival has recently concluded its final season,
       marking the end of an era in Fair Oaks’ cultural history. The District wishes
       to formally recognize FOTF’s outstanding service and express gratitude to
       its founders, leaders, artists, and supporters.

       The attached Resolution (Attachment A) has been prepared for adoption
       by the Board of Directors to honor the Festival’s legacy and contributions.

IV.    Financial Analysis
       There is no financial impact associated with this recognition.

Respectfully Submitted,


Michael J. Aho
District Administrator
                                                                    PROCLAMATION
                                                             OF THE GOVERNING BOARD OF
                                               THE FAIR OAKS RECREATION AND PARK DISTRICT


                                                           County of Sacramento, State of California


                                                                        HONORING

                                                           Fair Oaks Theatre Festival
     RECOGNIZING THE FAIR OAKS THEATRE FESTIVAL FOR OVER FOUR DECADES OF DEDICATED SERVICE TO THE
                                       COMMUNITY OF FAIR OAKS




WHEREAS, the Fair Oaks Theatre Festival was founded over 40 years ago by a visionary group of local business leaders and community members
who saw promise in the underutilized Veterans Memorial Amphitheater; and

WHEREAS, under the leadership of founders Wendell and Lois Frazier, and continued by dedicated leaders such as Tony and Barbara Sestito, the Fair
Oaks Theatre Festival provided a platform for Shakespearean productions, community performances, educational programs, and mentorship for
emerging artists; and

WHEREAS, FAIR OAKS THEATRE FESTIVAL became a respected cultural institution in the Sacramento region, offering affordable, quality
theatrical performances and enriching the lives of thousands of residents and visitors alike; and

WHEREAS, the success and longevity of FAIR OAKS THEATRE FESTIVAL was made possible through the tireless efforts of passionate
volunteers, directors, designers, actors, technicians, board members, and community supporters; and

WHEREAS, countless individuals, families, and organizations have supported and contributed to the Fair Oaks Theatre Festival’s mission,
including American River College, the Fair Oaks Rotary Club, Fair Oaks Chamber of Commerce, Fair Oaks Brew Pub, and the Fair Oaks Recreation and
Park District itself; and

WHEREAS, the Fair Oaks Theatre Festival has officially closed its curtain for the final time, marking the end of a beloved and influential era in
Fair Oaks history;

NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Board of Directors of the Fair Oaks Recreation and Park District does hereby extend its deepest
appreciation and heartfelt recognition to the Fair Oaks Theatre Festival, its founders, leaders, supporters, and artists for their outstanding
contributions to the cultural and community life of Fair Oaks.

BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the legacy of the Fair Oaks Theatre Festival shall be forever remembered as a cherished part of the
community’s identity and cultural history.

PASSED AND ADOPTED this 17th day of September, 2025.
