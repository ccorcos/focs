SACRAMENTO COUNTY BOARD OF EDUCATION
Declaring an Election to Be Held in Its Jurisdiction; Requesting the Board of
Supervisors of Sacramento and Placer Counties to Consolidate with Any Other
Election Conducted on Said Date; and Requesting Election Services by the
Registrars of Voters of Sacramento and Placer Counties
Resolution No. 25-16

WHEREAS, under Education Code sections 1007 and 5322, it is the determination of
the Sacramento County Board of Education that an Election be held on June 2, 2026,
for the purpose of electing three (3) members to the governing board of the Sacramento
County Board of Education for the following Trustee Areas and terms of office:

      Trustee Area I
      Said Term: 4 years
      (07/01/2026 – 06/30/2030)

      Trustee Area II
      Said Term: 4 years
      (07/01/2026 – 06/30/2030)

      Trustee Area III
      Said Term: 4 years
      (07/01/2026 – 06/30/2030)

WHEREAS, under Education Code section 1006, any registered voter living within the
boundaries of the county board of education is eligible to be a member of the county
board of education, except the county superintendent of schools, any member of his or
her staff, or any employee of a school district that is within the jurisdiction of the county
board of education.

WHEREAS, Trustee Areas I and III are fully within Sacramento County, and Trustee
Area II is primarily within Sacramento County, and partially in Placer County.

WHEREAS, County board of education members residing in each trustee area are
elected by the registered voters of that particular trustee area.

NOW THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, that for said election, the candidate’s
statement of qualifications shall be limited to 200 (or 400) words.

BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that all candidates, including incumbents, shall be
responsible for and provide for the prepayment of the publication cost of the voluntary
candidate’s statement, under Elections Code sections 13307, 13307.7, and 13308.
Prepayment shall be made at the time the candidate(s) file their nomination documents
at the Sacramento County Voter Registration and Elections Office, located at 7000 65th
Street, Suite A, Sacramento, CA, and at the time Trustee Area II candidate(s) submit a
voluntary candidate’s statement form pursuant to Elections Code section 13307.7 to the
Placer County Clerk-Recorder-Elections located at 3715 Atherton Road, Rocklin, CA
95765. Candidates determined by the County Elections Office to be indigent under
Elections Code section 13309 and Business and Professions Code section 6213,
subdivision (d), shall not be required to prepay for such publication costs, however, at

BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that if a tie vote makes it impossible to determine which
of two or more candidates has been elected to the governing board, the winner shall be
determined by lot, method to be determined by the Sacramento County Registrar of
Voters.

BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that there have been no changes to county board of
education trustee area boundary lines since the last election.

BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that the Boards of Supervisors of the Counties of
Sacramento and Placer are requested to:

    1. Consolidate said election with any other applicable election conducted on the
       same day;
    2. Authorize and direct the County Elections Office, at governing body expense, to
       provide all necessary election services which shall include, but not be limited to:
              Publish notices of election, including a combined election notice with other
              districts; issue nomination papers; check petition signatures; print and mail
              voter information guides; print official ballots; provide voting equipment
              supplies; appoint and train election officers; obtain and rent vote centers;
              prepare voter indexes; publish lists of election officers and vote centers;
              mail appropriate notification of vote centers and ballot drop-off locations;
              process vote-by-mail ballots; provide voter count by precinct; and canvass
              the results of said election.

    3. This Resolution shall be considered a Notice of Election and Specification of
       Election Order if applicable.
