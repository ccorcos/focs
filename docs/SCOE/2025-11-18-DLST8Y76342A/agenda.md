[[**BACKGROUND:**]{style="font-family:Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif;"}]{style="font-size:14px;"}

[[[[The County Superintendent of Schools is required to submit a report
each November to the County Board of Education describing the state of
the schools in the county that have been designated as Williams Schools
by the State Superintendent of Public Instruction (SSPI). The list
includes those schools 1) eligible for Comprehensive Support and
Improvement under Every Student Succeeds Act (ESSA); 2) eligible for
Additional Targeted Support and Improvement under ESSA; or 3) in which
15 percent or more of the school's teachers do not possess a valid and
clear or preliminary teaching credential. (Ed. Code, § 1240.) For the
2024-2025 school year, county superintendents used the 2021-2024 list of
Williams schools, and the SSPI released a new list in April 2025 for
2025-2027. ]{style="box-sizing: inherit; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-optical-sizing: inherit; font-size-adjust: inherit; font-kerning: inherit; font-feature-settings: inherit; font-variation-settings: inherit;"}]{style="box-sizing: inherit; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-optical-sizing: inherit; font-size-adjust: inherit; font-kerning: inherit; font-feature-settings: inherit; font-variation-settings: inherit;"}]{style="font-family:Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif;"}]{style="font-size:14px;"}

[[[[The attached report summarizes the results of Williams reviews
conducted during the 2024-2025 school year. For additional details
regarding these reviews, please refer to the Annual Williams reports
