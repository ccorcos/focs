[[**[Program
Description: ]{arial=""}**]{style="font-family:Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif;"}]{style="font-size:14px;"}

[The California Department of Education (CDE) has awarded a contract to
Educational Testing Services (ETS) for five administrations of the
California Assessment of Student Performance and Progress (CAASPP) and
the English Language Proficiency Assessments for California (ELPAC)
systems, collectively known as the California Assessment
System. ]{style="font-size: 14px;"}

 

[ETS has subcontracted with the Sacramento County Office of Education
for the following related to California
assessments: ]{style="font-size: 14px;"}

- [Recruit educators for educator review
  meetings]{style="font-size: 14px;"}
- [Provide training to LEAs and test
  examiners]{style="font-size: 14px;"}
- [Compose testing materials]{style="font-size: 14px;"}
- [Assist with key scoring activities]{style="font-size: 14px;"}

 

[[**[[New Positions: 
]{style="color:black"}]{arial=""}**[[[[[[None]{style="color:black"}]{arial=""}]{style="font-family:Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif;"}]{style="font-size:14px;"}]{style="color:black"}]{arial=""}]{style="font-family:Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif;"}]{style="font-size:14px;"}

 

[[**[[Subcontracts:]{style="color:black"}]{arial=""}**]{style="font-family:Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif;"}]{style="font-size:14px;"}

- [Iron Mountain Films, Inc. in the amount of \$2,510,637 for specific
  filming, audio and video production.]{style="font-size: 14px;"}
- [Transcend Translations, Inc. in the amount of \$100,000 for
  translation of test materials, parent letters, and video
  scripts.]{style="font-size: 14px;"}
- [Tech For All, Inc. in the amount of \$680,555 to provide
  accessibility services for tagging of testing materials, and video
  scripts.]{style="font-size: 14px;"}
- [Caption Max in the amount of \$250,000 to provide closed captioning
  services for webinars and training audio and
  videos.]{style="font-size: 14px;"}
- [Litigation Services in the amount of \$125,000 to provide
  transcription services for secure testing content, in particular
  student speaking responses for the ELPAC.]{style="font-size: 14px;"}

 

[[**[[Evaluation Component:  ]{style="color:black"}]{arial=""}**CSAPA
project management staff monitors  progress regularly through meetings
with ETS and CDE project management
staff. ]{style="font-size:14px;"}]{style="font-family:Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif;"}
