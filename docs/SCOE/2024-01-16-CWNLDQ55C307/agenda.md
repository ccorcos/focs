[[[Under the direction of the State Board of Education (SBE), the
Sacramento County Office of Education (SCOE) will receive \$100,000 from
the California Department of Education (CDE) ]{style="color:black"}to
update the 2021 *California Digital Learning Integration and Standards
Guidance* (DLISG) on mathematics to be in alignment with the SBE-adopted
*[Mathematics Framework for California Public Schools: Kindergarten
Through Grade Twelve]{style="color:black"}*[ (*Mathematics
Framework*)]{style="color:black"}.]{style="font-family:Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif;"}]{style="font-size:14px;"}

 

[[[SCOE will fulfill five project tasks: (1) Research existing support
practices and draft a 2024 DLISG project outline and budget; (2) Review
and assess the current (2021) DLISG and identify
]{style="color:black"}inconsistencies, or insufficient guidance
requiring revision or removal to align with the guidance in the approved
*Mathematics Framework*; (3) Prepare draft amendments to Section B:
Standards Guidance for Mathematics of the DLISG that will be reviewed by
the SBE; (4) Present proposed amendments to the SBE; and (5) Edit,
prepare, and submit the final accessible version of the SBE-adopted
DLISG.]{style="font-family:Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif;"}]{style="font-size:14px;"}
