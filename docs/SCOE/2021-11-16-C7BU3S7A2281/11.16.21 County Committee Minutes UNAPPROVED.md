6. NEW BUSINESS

Public Hearing and Consideration of Potential Adjustment of Sacramento County Board of Education Trustee Areas
Based Upon 2020 Census

SCOE General Counsel, Teresa Stinson, and the demographers, Justin Rich and Andrew Rich from Cooperative
Strategies, presented this item, provided background and history regarding the development of the Board’s
existing trustee area boundaries after the 2010 Census, reviewed the three alternative trustee area map
scenarios that would further balance population among the Board’s trustee areas, and sought County
Committee input and direction.

The public hearing was opened at 8:36 p.m.
