has consulted with various facilities and California libraries as they
plan to expand libraries in Juvenile Justice spaces --- an opportunity
to pay forward what she's learned. Jessie takes time and effort to reach
students at El Centro using data, student interest, prior knowledge,
background, and culture to affirm her students and to provide pathways
for student achievement. She is the epitome of a life-long learner and a

students' lives that will sustain them for years to
