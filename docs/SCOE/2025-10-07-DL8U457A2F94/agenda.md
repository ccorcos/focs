Description: **]{style="font-family:Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif;"}]{style="font-size:14px;"}

[[SCOE\'s School of Education Department serves as the 21st Century
California School Leadership Regional Academy for the Placer-Sacramento
Geographic Region supporting 14 counties. The   Academy provides
high-quality professional development to support quality educational
leadership which is offered to central office administrative leaders,
school site administrative leaders, and   teacher leaders. Professional
learning focuses on leadership development skills such as using data to
inform decisions, understanding resources to support continuous
improvement, building   teams to guide leadership, and building
understanding of the impact of high-quality leadership to support
continuous school improvement and equity. The underlying foundation is
to support leaders'   capacity to lead for equity-driven continuous
improvement.]{style="font-family:Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif;"}]{style="font-size:14px;"}

 

[[SCOE\'s Regional Academy will continue to provide professional
development by establishing five sub-regions, with a lead county office
in each region, to directly support the leadership   development needs
for each region. The lead county offices will assist in identifying the
unique needs to support leadership development, and provide the ongoing
coaching needed to assist with   the implementation of programs and
practices for continuous school
improvement.]{style="font-family:Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif;"}]{style="font-size:14px;"}

 

[[**New Positions:**
None]{style="font-family:Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif;"}]{style="font-size:14px;"}

 

[[**Subcontracts:**]{style="font-family:Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif;"}]{style="font-size:14px;"}

[[SCOE will enter into subcontracts, as necessary, to ensure the
successful implementation of the grant. The subcontracts will support
the completion of tasks outlined in the grant\'s scope of work   and
will help fulfill all deliverables in alignment with the expectations
and requirements set forth by the
grantor.]{style="font-family:Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif;"}]{style="font-size:14px;"}

 

[[** Evaluation
Component:**]{style="font-family:Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif;"}]{style="font-size:14px;"}

[[** **Participants will evaluate program components including, but not
limited to, program delivery, level of knowledge gained, and degrees of
concept implementation. School districts and county offices   will rate
the effectiveness of the training and the level of continuous
improvement to the region. The Regional Academy will be evaluated on an
annual basis by the California Department of   Education on the
effectiveness of programs provided in the region and alignment to grant
requirements.]{style="font-family:Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif;"}]{style="font-size:14px;"}
