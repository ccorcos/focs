SACRAMENTO COUNTY COMMITTEE ON SCHOOL DISTRICT ORGANIZATION

Resolution to Adopt Redistricting Plan for the Sacramento County Board of
Education Trustee Areas Based on 2020 Census
Resolution No. CC-21-03

WHEREAS, pursuant to Education Code section 1002, subdivision (b), after each
decennial federal census, county board of education trustee areas must be assessed
and potentially adjusted in order to meet legally required population criteria.

WHEREAS, the Sacramento County Board of Education (Board) contracted with
Cooperative Strategies, LLC (Demographer) for redistricting services and expertise.

WHEREAS, population data from the 2020 Census was analyzed and used to suggest
potential adjustments to Trustee Areas in order to ensure compliance with state and
federal law.

WHEREAS, at the Board’s July 13, 2021 public meeting, the Demographer reviewed
the standards and process for adjusting trustee area boundaries; and on October 19,
2021, November 16, 2021, and December 14, 2021, the Board, in its capacity as
Sacramento County Committee on School District Organization (County Committee),
conducted public hearings to review and consider potential trustee area adjustment
scenarios and receive public input.

WHEREAS, Sacramento County Office of Education (SCOE) staff publicized the
Board’s trustee area redistricting process and solicited public comment on the Board's
current trustee area boundaries and the alternative trustee area map scenarios through
postings on SCOE’s public website, social media postings, news releases sent to 23
different television and print media outlets throughout the region and the thirteen local
school districts, a public notice published in the Sacramento Bee, and communications
with SCOE’s employee associations (California School Employees Association and
Sacramento County Office of Education Teachers Association).

IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED that:

    1. The recitals set forth above are true and correct.

    2. The Sacramento County Committee on School District Organization hereby
       adopts the attached Trustee Areas map scenario No. ___ as the redistricting plan
       for the Sacramento County Board of Education Trustee Areas based upon the
       2020 Census.

    3. The attached final redistricting plan shall be submitted to the County Registrars
       of Voters in Sacramento, San Joaquin, Solano, Placer, and Yolo Counties for the
       June 7, 2022 Board election and subsequent elections.

    4. The Sacramento County Committee on School District Organization hereby
       authorizes the Sacramento County Superintendent of Schools or designee to
       take any other actions needed to effectuate the purposes of this resolution.
