THE ASSOCIATION OF CALIFORNIA COUNTY BOARDS OF EDUCATION (ACCBE) is an
independent nonprofit organization dedicated to serving and empowering County Boards of
Education. Founded by county trustees for county trustees, ACCBE is committed to amplifying
the collective voice of county boards, strengthening board leadership, championing equity
and excellence, and driving better outcomes for students with the greatest needs.
