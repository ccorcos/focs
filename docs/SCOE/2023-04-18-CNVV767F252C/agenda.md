[[Eziuche Okemiri, Resource Specialist with the Special Education
Department, was nominated by Community Schools Principal Lisa Alcalá.
Dr. Eziuche Okemiri is the Resource Specialist for Community Schools.
Dr. O, as he is known to students and staff, works with students who
receive special education services. He helps them set both long-term and
short-term goals, as well as provides scaffolding to help students be
successful in the classroom. Dr. O also works with our families,
ensuring that parents and guardians are aware of all the tools that are
available to them as the family of a special needs child. He
communicates clearly and often, helping families navigate a system that
is sometimes confusing. As a colleague, Dr. O is always willing to help
wherever he is needed. With a bright smile and friendly demeanor, Dr. O
helps to create an inclusive community within our schools. Eziuche
Okemiri has been a SCOE employee since March
2008.]{style="font-family:Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif;"}]{style="font-size:14px;"}
