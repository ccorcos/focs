SACRAMENTO COUNTY BOARD OF EDUCATION
In-Support of the 2022-2023 United Way Campaign
Resolution No. 23-03


WHEREAS, the United Way is an organization dedicated to public service through the
efforts of hundreds of programs serving Sacramento, Placer, El Dorado, Amador, and
Yolo Counties in the areas of health and human-care service needs and concerns of
people of all ages, including family counseling, programs for youth, support and care for
the frail and elderly, and services for the disabled; and

WHEREAS, the United Way is a vehicle through which people may help one another
become fully contributing members of society; and

WHEREAS, through United Way agencies in the region, one out of three families in the
community will be helped by a United Way agency this year; and

WHEREAS, the United Way is dependent upon community contributions to finance the
programs offered to those in need through those agencies; and

WHEREAS, the United Way fund-raising campaign is a community project conducted
largely through the efforts of dedicated volunteers; and

WHEREAS, United Way supports programs and initiatives that support children's
health, foster youth services, child abuse prevention and intervention, and helping teens
succeed, as well as partnering with over 150 certificated agencies which meet stringent
guidelines for giving; and

WHEREAS, many Sacramento County students, as well as some Sacramento County
Office of Education programs, are among those assisted by agencies funded by United
Way;

NOW, THEREFORE LET IT BE RESOLVED that the Sacramento County Board of
Education does hereby proclaim May 1 - 31, 2023, as United Way Campaign Month and
urges all community-minded citizens to give the United Way their full support through
financial and/or other voluntary means.

PASSED AND ADOPTED by the Sacramento County Board of Education at its regular
Board meeting on the 18th day of April 2023 by the following vote:
