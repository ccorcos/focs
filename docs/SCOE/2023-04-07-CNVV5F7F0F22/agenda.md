[[**Program
Description: **]{style="font-family:Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif;"}]{style="font-size:14px;"}

[[The Sacramento County Office of Education (SCOE) School of Education
serves as the 21st Century California School Leadership Regional Academy
for the Placer-Sacramento Geographic Region. The Academy provides
high-quality professional development to support quality educational
leadership. This professional development will be provided to central
office administrative leaders, school site administrative leaders, and
teacher leaders. Professional learning will focus on leadership
development skills such as using data to inform decisions, understanding
resources to support continuous improvement, building teams to guide
leadership, and building understanding of the impact of high-quality
leadership to support continuous school improvement. The underlying
foundation is to support leaders' capacity to lead for equity-driven
continuous
improvement.]{style="font-family:Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif;"}]{style="font-size:14px;"}

 

[[The SCOE Regional Academy will continue to provide professional
development through establishing five sub-regions, with a lead county
office in each region, to assist with directly supporting the leadership
development needs for each region. The lead county offices will assist
in identifying the unique needs to support leadership development, and
provide the ongoing coaching needed to assist with the implementation of
programs and practices for continuous school
improvement.]{style="font-family:Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif;"}]{style="font-size:14px;"}

Positions: ** ]{style="font-family:Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif;"}]{style="font-size:14px;"}

- [[Coordinator]{style="font-family:Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif;"}]{style="font-size:14px;"}

- [[Administrative
  Assistant]{style="font-family:Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif;"}]{style="font-size:14px;"}

 

[[**Subcontracts:**]{style="font-family:Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif;"}]{style="font-size:14px;"}

- [[Creative Leadership Solutions to provide professional development
  and coaching in the area of equity and continuous
  improvement.]{style="font-family:Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif;"}]{style="font-size:14px;"}
- [[Fire Horse Rising (Dr. Nancy Dome) to provide professional
  development in the area of equity and continuous
  improvement.]{style="font-family:Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif;"}]{style="font-size:14px;"}
- [[Various subcontracts with professional learning providers as
  identified through our program needs assessment.
  ]{style="font-family:Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif;"}]{style="font-size:14px;"}
- [[Various inter-program contracts with Communications, Research and
  Evaluation, Printing Services, and the Leadership
  Institute.]{style="font-family:Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif;"}]{style="font-size:14px;"}

 

[[**Evaluation: **]{style="font-family:Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif;"}]{style="font-size:14px;"}

[[Evaluation of the professional learning will be completed by
participant evaluations of program components including, but not limited
to, program delivery, level of knowledge gained, and degrees of concept
implementation. School districts and county offices will rate the
effectiveness of the training and the level of continuous improvement to
the
region.]{style="font-family:Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif;"}]{style="font-size:14px;"}

 

[[The Regional Academy will be evaluated on an annual basis by the
California Department of Education. This evaluation will include areas
of level of effectiveness of program provided in the region and
alignment to meeting grant
requirements.]{style="font-family:Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif;"}]{style="font-size:14px;"}
