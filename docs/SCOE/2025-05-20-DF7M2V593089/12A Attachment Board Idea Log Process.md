                                     Board Member “Idea Log” Process

In response to the Sacramento County Board of Education’s (Board) request, future
public Board meeting agendas will include an agenda item where Board members may
voice suggestions and ideas on future Board agenda items. To comply with the
limitations and requirements of the Brown Act, Board member’s ideas will be briefly
stated and will not be voted upon or substantively discussed. The ideas will be noted for
follow-up and placed on the “Idea Log” going forward.

The Board President and Board Secretary/Superintendent shall subsequently deliberate
on the Idea Log and coordinate action, considering Board interest and available staff
resources, and may:

(1) consider placing an item on a future Board agenda for either:

         (a) presentation by staff or external partners

         (b) for information, discussion or action.

(2) refer an item to staff for additional information or follow-up from staff directly to the
Trustee that requested the information.

Pursuant to Board Bylaw 9122, the Board President or Board Secretary/Superintendent
may request the assistance of the Executive Committee in determining matters to be
placed on the Board agenda.
