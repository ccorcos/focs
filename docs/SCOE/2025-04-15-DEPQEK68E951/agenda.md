[[**Program
Description:**]{style="font-family:Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif;"}]{style="font-size:14px;"}

[[This project aims to strengthen professional learning support for
educators across Sacramento County, equipping them with the knowledge,
resources, and strategies to foster meaningful math experiences that
build student confidence, problem-solving skills, and deep conceptual
understanding. Through professional learning and coaching, educators
will create inclusive environments that inspire curiosity, perseverance,
and a lifelong appreciation for mathematics. To achieve this, SCOE and
PowerMyLearning (PML) will develop a plan to dentify key shifts in
instructional practice and establish data points to evaluate
professional learning effectiveness. To develop the plan, we will
collect and review relevant policies, regulations, and data on math
instruction and professional development, and analyze this information
to map current practices and establish a baseline for instructional
improvement. Within 90 days, we will finalize process mapping and
develop a high-impact professional learning and implementation plan for
Sacramento County that is aligned with the California Math Framework.
The goal of this grant is to create a comprehensive three-year strategy,
ensuring sustainable professional learning support that directly
enhances student
outcomes. ]{style="font-family:Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif;"}]{style="font-size:14px;"}
