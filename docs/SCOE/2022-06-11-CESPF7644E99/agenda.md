[[**BACKGROUND:**]{style="font-family:Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif;"}]{style="font-size:14px;"}

 

[[The governing board of the Galt Joint Union High School District
(GJUHSD) has adopted and submitted a proposal to change the method of
electing GJUHSD governing board members. (Ed. Code, § 5019(a), (c).)
Currently, the five GJUHSD board members are elected under an
\"at-large" system, where each trustee is elected by the registered
voters of the entire school district. GJUHSD proposes, effective for the
November 2022 and 2024 election cycles, to establish trustee areas,
change its election method so one board member residing within each
trustee area will be elected by the registered voters residing in that
trustee area ("by-trustee area" elections), and establish the sequence
of elections (proposal).
]{style="font-family:Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif;"}]{style="font-size:14px;"}

 

[[GJUHSD board members are elected in even-numbered years and serve
staggered, four-year terms. The next election for three GJUHSD board
members is scheduled for November 2022, and the election for the
remaining two board members is scheduled for November 2024. GJUHSD
proposes to use the same sequence of elections so that three trustee
areas are assigned for election in 2022 and two trustee areas are
assigned for election in 2024.
]{style="font-family:Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif;"}]{style="font-size:14px;"}

 

[[By law, the Sacramento County Committee on School District
Organization (County Committee) must call and conduct a hearing within
the district on GJUHSD's proposal and, at the conclusion of its hearing,
approve or disapprove GJUHSD\'s proposal. (Ed. Code, §
5019(c)(2).)]{style="font-family:Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif;"}]{style="font-size:14px;"}

[[The governing board of the Galt Joint Union Elementary School District
(GJUESD) has adopted and submitted a proposal to change the method of
electing GJUESD governing board members. (Ed. Code, § 5019(a), (c).)
Currently, the five GJUESD board members are elected under an
\"at-large" system, where each trustee is elected by the registered
voters of the entire school district. GJUESD proposes, effective for the
November 2022 and 2024 election cycles, to establish trustee areas,
change its election method so one board member residing within each
trustee area will be elected by the registered voters residing in that
trustee area ("by-trustee area" elections), and establish the sequence
of elections (proposal).
]{style="font-family:Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif;"}]{style="font-size:14px;"}

 

[[GJUESD board members are elected in even-numbered years and serve
staggered, four-year terms. The next election for three GJUESD board
members is scheduled for November 2022, and the election for the
remaining two board members is scheduled for November 2024. GJUESD
proposes to use the same sequence of elections so that three trustee
areas are assigned for election in 2022 and two trustee areas are
assigned for election in 2024.
]{style="font-family:Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif;"}]{style="font-size:14px;"}

 

[[By law, the Sacramento County Committee on School District
Organization (County Committee) must call and conduct a hearing within
