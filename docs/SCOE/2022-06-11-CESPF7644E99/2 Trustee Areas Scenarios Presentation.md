    SCENARIO 1 - CONSIDERATIONS


•    Scenario 1 was drawn with a focus on the City of Galt as this is the most densely
     Hispanic/Latino portion of the district.

•    Uses main roads and man-made features as boundaries where possible.

•    Uses Feeder District boundaries and High School boundaries to inform the Trustee
     Area boundaries.

•    North Galt must be split into two areas (population is too large).

•    There is one unoccupied Trustee Area (Area 2).

•    There is one Trustee Area that has two Trustees paired up (Area 1).
       • Both reach term expiration in 2022.

•    Trustee Area 2 holds the largest Hispanic/Latino CVAP of 44.8%.

•    Overall variance is 4.2%.

    CONCEPTUAL TRUSTEE AREAS
    SCENARIO 3 - CONSIDERATIONS


•    Scenario 3 was drawn with a focus on compact boundaries & overall variance using
     roads/railways as primary dividers.

•    Keeping the boundary lines neater/cleaner and keeping the areas more compact
     resulted in a lower Hispanic/Latino CVAP than the other scenarios, but with the best
     overall variance amongst the scenarios.

•    Uses Feeder District boundaries and High School boundaries to inform the Trustee
     Area boundaries.

•    There is one unoccupied Trustee Area (Area 2).

•    There is one Trustee Area that has two Trustees paired up (Area 1).
       • Both reach term expiration in 2022.

•    Trustee Area 2 holds the largest Hispanic/Latino CVAP of 36.1%.

•    Overall variance is the lowest of all scenarios at 1.6%.
