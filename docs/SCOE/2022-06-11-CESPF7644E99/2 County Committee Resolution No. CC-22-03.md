SACRAMENTO COUNTY COMMITTEE ON SCHOOL DISTRICT ORGANIZATION
Resolution Approving Galt Joint Union Elementary School District’s Proposal
Under Education Code Section 5019 to Change to “By-Trustee Area” Elections,
Adopt Trustee Areas Map, and Establish Sequence of Elections
Resolution No. CC-22-03

WHEREAS, the governing board of the Galt Joint Union Elementary School District
(GJUESD) is composed of five members, who are elected under an “at-large” election
system, where each trustee is elected by the registered voters of the entire school
district;

WHEREAS, Education Code section 5019 authorizes a school district governing board,
by resolution, to change its method of electing governing board members;

WHEREAS, Education Code section 5030 authorizes alternative methods of electing
school district governing board members, including at-large, hybrid, and by-trustee area
elections;

WHEREAS, on May 12, 2022, GJUESD submitted a proposal to the Sacramento
County Committee on School District Organization (County Committee) under
Education Code section 5019 to, effective for the November 2022 and 2024 election
cycles, establish trustee areas, change its election method so one board member
residing within each trustee area will be elected by the registered voters residing in that
trustee area (“by-trustee area” elections), and establish the sequence of elections
(proposal).

WHEREAS, GJUESD’s proposal and public website and other publicly available
information indicate the following history, public hearings outreach, and other events
related to GJUESD’s proposed change of election method:

    •    February 9, 2022 – GJUESD adopted Resolution No. 8 expressing its intention to
         transition to a by-trustee area election system to be implemented with the
         November 2022 and 2024 governing board election cycles.

    •    February 15, 2022 to May 2, 2022 – GJUESD met with stakeholders, including
         District Advisory Committee, District English Learner Advisory Committee,
         California School Employees Association, Galt Elementary Faculty Association,
         non-represented employees, and City and Schools Together regarding the
         proposed transition to by-trustee area elections. It also communicated regarding
         its process and proposed trustee area maps through multiple platforms, including
         through announcements on its public website and a dedicated webpage, through
         its Blackboard Connect Mass Notification System to all district staff and
         parents/guardians, community newsletters, and published notices in the Galt
         Herald and Lodi News Sentinel.

    •    February 23 and March 9, 2022 – GJUESD held public hearings to consider
         community input and comment regarding the potential composition of trustee
         areas prior to draft maps being drawn. At its March 9, 2022 special board
         meeting, GJUESD presented a timeline for transition to by-trustee area elections.

WHEREAS, on June 11, 2022, as required by Education Code section 5019, and after
having complied with all notice requirements, the County Committee conducted a public
hearing in the district on GJUESD’s proposal to change to by-trustee area elections,
which was also available and accessible within GJUESD and throughout the community
via Zoom video and/or audio teleconference;

WHEREAS, notice of the County Committee’s public hearing location, including video
and/or audio teleconference hearing access, was posted on the GJUESD and
Sacramento County Office of Education websites.

WHEREAS, GJUESD has submitted information in support of its proposal to change its
election method to “by-trustee area” elections, the record reflects rational reasons for
GJUESD’s proposal, and GJUESD’s proposal appears to be consistent with the law.

WHEREAS, GJUESD 's proposal and supporting materials demonstrate that GJUESD
initiated its proposed change to by-trustee area elections in furtherance of the California
Voting Rights Act of 2001 (CVRA), and the County Committee declares that GJUESD’s
change in election method is being made in furtherance of the purposes of the CVRA.

NOW, THEREFORE, IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED that:

    1. The recitals set forth above are correct and true.

    2. The Sacramento County Committee on School District Organization hereby
       approves GJUESD’s proposal to:
          a. Adopt by-trustee area elections, implementing GJUESD’s trustee area
             boundaries map identified as Map B.
          b. Assign the sequence of elections for GJUESD’s five trustee areas as
             follows:
                  i. Trustee Areas 1, 2 and 5 scheduled for election in 2022; and
                 ii. Trustee Areas 3 and 4 scheduled for election in 2024.

    3. GJUESD shall submit the change in method of electing governing board
       members, trustee areas boundaries map, sequence of elections, and any other
       required information to the appropriate county elections officials, and to all other
       necessary parties by all required deadlines.
