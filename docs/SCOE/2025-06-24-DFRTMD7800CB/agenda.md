[[**BACKGROUND:**]{style="font-family:Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif;"}]{style="font-size:14px;"}

 

[[The 2025--2026 Course Catalog reflects our commitment to providing a
rigorous, inclusive, and equitable set of learning pathways for all
students. This catalog includes a wide range of academic and elective
offerings that support college and career readiness. Key components of
this year's catalog
include:]{style="font-family:Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif;"}]{style="font-size:14px;"}

 

[[**A-G Approved Courses**\
The catalog features updated University of California (UC)/California
State University (CSU) A-G approved courses, ensuring students have
access to the coursework necessary to meet entrance requirements for
public universities in California. All subject areas (A--G) are
represented.
]{style="font-family:Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif;"}]{style="font-size:14px;"}

 

[[**Career Technical Education (CTE)**\
The catalog highlights CTE courses aligned to California\'s 15 industry
sectors. Courses offer real-world skills, project-based learning, and
industry certifications where applicable. These CTE offerings include
Business (Entrepreneurship), Construction, Culinary, and Horticulture.
Work-based learning opportunities are integrated when
possible.]{style="font-family:Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif;"}]{style="font-size:14px;"}

 

[[**Dual Enrollment**\
In collaboration with Los Rios Community College, the catalog expands
Dual Enrollment options for high school students. These courses provide
both high school and college credit, supporting early college access and
reducing the time and cost for postsecondary degree completion. Courses
are aligned with student interests and workforce trends, including
general education and career-focused
pathways.]{style="font-family:Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif;"}]{style="font-size:14px;"}

 

[[**Online Learning - Edgenuity**\
To support flexible and personalized learning, the catalog appendix
includes Edgenuity online course offerings. These courses meet
California standards and offer credit recovery, grade improvement, and
original credit opportunities. All Edgenuity courses are reviewed for
rigor and alignment with district graduation and A-G requirements when
applicable. Courses will be added once approved through UC Doorways and
added to our Student Information
System.]{style="font-family:Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif;"}]{style="font-size:14px;"}

[[ ]{style="font-family:Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif;"}]{style="font-size:14px;"}

[[At the May 29, 2025 Curriculum Committee meeting, the 2025-2026 Course
Catalog was reviewed, and the Committee recommended this catalog be sent
forward for Board approval and adoption at the June 24, 2025 Board
meeting.
]{style="font-family:Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif;"}]{style="font-size:14px;"}

[[ ]{style="font-family:Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif;"}]{style="font-size:14px;"}

[[**Next Steps**\
Pending Board approval, the catalog will be published, translated into
multiple languages, and shared with school counselors, administrators,
and families to support course selection for the 2025--2026 academic
year.]{style="font-family:Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif;"}]{style="font-size:14px;"}
