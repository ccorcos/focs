SACRAMENTO COUNTY BOARD OF EDUCATION
Resolution Denying the Capital College & Career Academy Countywide Charter
Petition
Resolution No. 22-07

WHEREAS, on February 25, 2022, the Capital College & Career Academy (Petitioner
or CCCA) submitted a petition for a countywide charter school to the Sacramento
County Board of Education (County Board) and submitted supplemental information
and documents on March 3 and 9, 2022;

WHEREAS, CCCA’s charter petition was analyzed by SCOE staff with expertise in
each of the petition areas (fiscal, educational programs, student assessments, special
education, English learners, human resources, facilities, and legal) and Sacramento
County Office of Education (SCOE) staff prepared written Recommendations and
Findings for consideration by the County Board;

WHEREAS, on March 28, 2022, SCOE’s written Recommendations and Findings
were published on SCOE’s public website and provided to CCCA and each of the
school districts in Sacramento County.

WHEREAS, on April 12, 2022, the County Board held a public hearing to consider the
level of public support for CCCA’s petition, hear testimony from CCCA, SCOE staff,
and the public, and CCCA representatives had equivalent time and procedures to
present evidence and testimony to respond to SCOE’s Recommendations and
Findings;

WHEREAS, on April 12, 2022, following review of the petition and the public hearing
noted above, the County Board considered and deliberated upon CCCA’s countywide
charter petition;

NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that having reviewed CCCA’s petition,
conducted the public hearing, and considered and deliberated upon the petition, the
County Board adopts SCOE’s written Recommendations and Findings for the CCCA
countywide charter petition;

NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that having determined that there
is reasonable justification to deny the petition, CCCA’s countywide charter petition is
hereby denied.

PASSED AND ADOPTED by the Sacramento County Board of Education at its regular
Board meeting on the 12th day of April 2022 by the following vote:
