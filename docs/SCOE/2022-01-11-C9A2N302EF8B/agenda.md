[[**BACKGROUND:**]{style="font-family:Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif;"}]{style="font-size:14px;"}

 

[[[The governing board of the Natomas Unified School District (NUSD) has
adopted and submitted a proposal to change the method of electing NUSD
governing board members. (Ed. Code, § 5019(a), (c).) Currently, the
five NUSD board members are elected under an \"at-large" system, where
each trustee is elected by the registered voters of the entire school
district. NUSD proposes, effective for the November 2022 and 2024
election cycles, to change its election method so one ]{arial=""}board
member residing within each trustee area will be elected by the
registered voters residing in that trustee area ("by-trustee area"
elections).]{style="font-family:Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif;"}]{style="font-size:14px;"}

[[[NUSD board members are elected in even-numbered years and serve
staggered, four-year terms. The next election for two NUSD board members
is scheduled for November 2022, and the election for the remaining three
board members is scheduled for November 2024. NUSD proposes to sequence
its elections so that two trustee areas are assigned for election in
2022 and three trustee areas are assigned for election in
2024.]{arial=""}]{style="font-family:Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif;"}]{style="font-size:14px;"}

[[[By law, the Sacramento County Committee on School District
Organization (County Committee) must call and conduct a hearing on
NUSD's proposal and, at the conclusion of its hearing, approve or
disapprove NUSD's proposal. (Ed. Code, §
5019(c)(2).)]{arial=""}]{style="font-family:Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif;"}]{style="font-size:14px;"}
