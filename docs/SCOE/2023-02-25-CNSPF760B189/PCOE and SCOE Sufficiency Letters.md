                                Roseville City School District and Roseville Joint Union High School District.
                              ► To promote geographic compactness. For example, Westbrook Elementary
                                School is under construction a block from the Aspire neighborhood and can be
                                visualized from Aspire yet our children are unable to attend and instead are
                                expected to endure an unreasonable, prolonged commute to school.
                              ► It is in the best interest of the student population within our neighborhood.

                               The enclosed petition was submitted to the Placer and Sacramento County Offices
                               of Education by Melissa Campbell as Chief Petitioner. The petition seeks to
                               transfer the “Aspire Solaire” neighborhood (bordered by Daylight Drive, Solaire
                               Drive, Santucci Boulevard and Pleasant Grove Boulevard) in Roseville, California
                               from the Center Joint Unified School District to the Roseville City School District and
                               Roseville Joint Union High School District.

                               The Center school district is within the jurisdiction of the Sacramento County
                               Superintendent of Schools, while the Roseville school districts are within the
                               jurisdiction of the Placer County Superintendent of Schools. As the territory
                               proposed for transfer is within an area of the Center school district that is in Placer
                               County, the Placer County Superintendent of Schools has taken the lead in
                               validating the petition signatures.

                               On January 13, 2023, the Placer County Superintendent, in consultation with the
                               Placer County Elections Office, found the petition to be sufficient and signed as
                               required by law. As such, she transmitted the petition to the State Board of
                               Education and the Placer County Committee on School District Organization.

                               Based upon the petition and the signature certification information provided by the
                               Placer County Superintendent and Elections Office, I concur with the Placer
                               County Superintendent’s sufficiency determination. Therefore, we are transmitting
                               the petition to the State Board of Education and the Sacramento County
                               Committee on School District Organization, and we will comply with the
                               requirements set forth in Education Code sections 35705 et seq.
