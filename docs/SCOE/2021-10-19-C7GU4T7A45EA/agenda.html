A. Public Hearing and Consideration of Potential Adjustment of Sacramento County Board of Education Trustee Areas Based Upon 2020 Census - Teresa Stinson
Meeting
Oct 19, 2021 - SACRAMENTO COUNTY COMMITTEE ON SCHOOL DISTRICT ORGANIZATION
Category
6. NEW BUSINESS
Type
Action
Recommended Action
Consider and Provide Direction

BACKGROUND:

 

After each decennial federal census, county board of education trustee areas must be assessed and potentially adjusted in order to meet legally required population criteria. (Ed. Code, § 1002(b).) We have contracted with a demographer, Cooperative Strategies, LLC, to assist the Sacramento County Board of Education (Board), in its capacity as Sacramento County Committee on School District Organization (County Committee), to analyze 2020 census data and provide options for ensuring the Board's trustee areas are compliant with state and federal legal requirements.  

 

By law, the difference in population between the smallest and largest trustee area must be no more than 10%. Based upon assessment of the Board's trustee area population balance using 2020 Census data, the variance between the smallest and largest trustee areas is 8.3%, which is within the 10% threshold. Therefore, the County Committee may choose to maintain the Board's current trustee area boundaries, or make adjustments to further balance population.

 

NEXT STEPS:

 

At the October 19, 2021 meeting, Cooperative Strategies, LLC will review redistricting standards and the current trustee areas maps, provide options for potential readjustment scenarios, answer questions, and seek County Committee input and direction on maintaining or readjusting the Board's trustee area boundaries.

 

After receiving public comment and deliberating, the County Committee may express its intention to readopt the Board's current map, consider an alternative map, or seek additional options. Based upon the County Committee's input and direction, potential map(s) will be published and considered for adoption at a subsequent meeting(s).

 

Once the County Committee adopts the Board's final trustee areas map, the final map will be submitted to the County Registrar of Voters in Sacramento, San Joaquin, Solano, Placer, and Yolo counties. 

 

SUPERINTENDENT'S RECOMMENDATION:

 

The Superintendent recommends that the Sacramento County Committee on School District Organization review and consider the information provided by Cooperative Strategies, LLC and take action as it deems appropriate.
