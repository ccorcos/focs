SACRAMENTO COUNTY BOARD OF EDUCATION
Resolution Authorizing Five Percent Increase in Board Member Compensation
Under Education Code Section 1090
Resolution No. 21-18

WHEREAS, pursuant to Education Code section 1090(a)(2), Sacramento County Board
of Education (Board) members who attend all meetings in a month receive
compensation of $400.00 per month.

WHEREAS, pursuant to Education Code section 1090(g), on an annual basis, the
Board may increase its members’ present monthly compensation by up to 5 percent.

WHEREAS, according to information provided by 8 county boards of education in
similarly sized counties, their county board of education members receive between
$498.34 – $1010.79 per month as compensation for their services.

WHEREAS, based upon the survey information referenced above, Board members’
present monthly compensation is less than the compensation received by county board
of education members in other similarly sized counties.

WHEREAS, increasing Board members’ present monthly compensation by 5 percent
would increase members monthly compensation by $20.00, which is a reasonable cost
of living adjustment.

NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, for the reasons set forth above and as
authorized by Education Code section 1090(g), the Board hereby increases the monthly
compensation received by its members by 5 percent, from $400.00 to $420.00 per
month.

PASSED AND ADOPTED by the Sacramento County Board of Education at its regular
Board meeting on the 14th day of December 2021 by the following vote:
