       Standing Committee

       A standing committee is authorized, on a limited basis, in order to facilitate the County Board of
       Education decision-making process. The County Board of Education members are to be appointed
       annually by the Executive Committee President to the following standing committees:
           • BUDGET/AUDIT - to review annual budget of the S.C.O.E.Sacramento County Office of
             Education (SCOE) and to review the annual audit report of the organization.
           • POLICY - to formulate and review board policies.
           • CURRICULUM - to approve curriculum as required.

       Ad Hoc Committees

       Members of the Board may also be appointed to serve on Ad Hoc committees as needed. The
       specific charge to the committee and the length of time they are to serve shall be stipulated at the
       time of appointment.

       Executive Committee

       Board appointments shall be made annually by tThe Executive Committee, which is hereby
       established. The Executive Committee shall consist of the vice-president, the president, and the
       immediate past president, who shall serve ex officio. The president of the County Board of Education
       shall serve as the chair. This committee also shall establish the agenda for each Board meeting if
       requested by the County Superintendent or Board President.
