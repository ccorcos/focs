                 Minutes of the Biennial Meeting of January 14, 2020

Agenda

I.     Call to Order/Roll Call/Establish Quorum
II.    Approval of Minutes of the Biennial Meeting of January 16, 2018
III.   Public Comments
IV.    Adjournment


I.         President Lefkovitz called the meeting to order at 8:51 p.m. in the Board
Room of the David P. Meaney Education Center, Sacramento County Office of
Education, 10474 Mather Boulevard, Mather, California. Members present were
Joanne Ahola, Al Brown, Heather Davis, Harold Fong, Paul Keefer, Bina Lefkovitz, and
Karina Talamantes. Also present were David Gordon, Secretary to the Board, and
other staff and visitors.

II.      Mr. Brown moved to accept the report of the minutes of the Biennial Meeting
of January 16, 2018. Ms. Davis seconded the motion. Motion carried unanimously (7
ayes).

III.      There were no public comments.

IV.      Ms. Ahola moved to adjourn the meeting. Mr. Keefer seconded the motion.
Motion carried unanimously (7 ayes). Meeting adjourned at 8:52 p.m.
