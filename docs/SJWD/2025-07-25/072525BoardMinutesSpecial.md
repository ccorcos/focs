IV.   DISCUSSION AND ACTION ITEMS
      1. Sacramento LAFCo Elections (W & R)
         President Costa reported that nominations are open for the Special District
         Representative seat. The Board discussed the open seats and Director Tobin
         expressed interest in being nominated.

          President Costa moved to nominate Director Pam Tobin for the regular
          position as Sacramento LAFCo Special District Representative for a 2-year
          term. Vice President McRae seconded the motion and it carried unanimously.
