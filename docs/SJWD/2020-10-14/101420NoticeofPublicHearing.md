                            SAN JUAN WATER DISTRICT
                            NOTICE OF PUBLIC HEARING


NOTICE IS HEREBY GIVEN that the Board of Directors of the San Juan Water District
will hold a public hearing both in-person at the District Office, 9935 Auburn Folsom Rd,
Granite Bay, CA 95746, and via videoconference in accordance with the Executive Orders
N-29-20 and N-25-20 regarding the COVID-19 emergency, on the following dates:

      Wednesday, October 14, 2020, at 6:00 p.m., or as soon thereafter as the matter may
      be heard. This public hearing will constitute the fourth and final public hearing of
      a series of four hearings the Board of Directors will hold pursuant to Elections Code
      section 10010 to receive public comments and input regarding the potential
      adoption of election division boundaries to elect the District's directors.

Concurrently with this notice, the proposed map of potential Board Member election
division boundaries and the proposed sequence of division elections have been published.
The Board will consider taking steps to adopt this map and sequence of division elections
at the hearing.

All interested parties are invited to attend the public hearing and express opinions or
submit information. The public hearing may be continued from time to time, as necessary,
by giving notice at the aforementioned hearing.

PARTICIPATION DURING COVID-19 PANDEMIC: Due to State guidelines on physical
distancing in public gatherings, the Boardroom can accommodate a maximum of 10
members of the public at one time. The District recommends that members of the public
participate in Board meetings via videoconference per the instructions below. Persons
who do plan to attend the meeting in person are urged to contact the Board Secretary
prior to the meeting. The contact information for the Board Secretary is:

PUBLIC COMMENTS: All interested parties are invited to attend the public hearing to
provide comments or send written comments to the Secretary for the Board, Teri Grant, at
the San Juan Water District Office, 9935 Auburn Folsom Rd, Granite Bay, CA 95746, or
via email to comments@sjwd.org, no later than the close of the public hearing.

The District does not transcribe its proceedings. Persons who wish to obtain a verbatim
record of the proceedings should arrange for attendance by a court reporter. Such
arrangements will be at the sole expense of the person requesting the recordation.

If you challenge the action taken on this matter in court, the challenge may be limited to
only those issues raised at the public hearings described in this notice, or in written
correspondence delivered to the Secretary for the Board at, or prior to, the public hearings.
