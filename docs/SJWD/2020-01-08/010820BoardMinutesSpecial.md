President Costa called the meeting to order at 6:00 p.m.

       The Board Secretary took a roll call of the Board. The following directors were present:

       President Costa introduced and announced that SSWD’s newly-elected President Kevin
       Thomas would like to continue with the 2x2 study on collaboration/integration and
       President Costa would like this to be put on an agenda. Mr. Thomas addressed the
       Board and agreed with President Costa’s comments.

       as needed via the General Manager or directly to the Board. President Costa requested

IV.   RECOGNITION OF STEVE NUGENT, CARMICHAEL WATER DISTRICT
      President Costa announced that Steve Nugent retired from Carmichael Water District
      and there is a resolution in recognition of his service.

      years of Service to the Water Community by Carmichael Water District General
      Manager, Steve Nugent. Director Hanneman seconded the motion and it carried
      unanimously.

V.    ADJOURN
      GM Helliker reported that he will be attending the Mid-Pacific Water Users’ Conference
      which conflicts with the January 22nd Board meeting and requested that the meeting be
      moved to January 23rd. President Costa inquired what agenda items were on the
      agenda for the next meeting – GM Helliker stated that the items include the Legal
      Counsel Evaluation, the GM Evaluation and Engineering Committee items from next
      week’s meeting, and Ms. Silva commented that there will be an introduction for an
      Ordinance amendment. Mr. Horowitz informed the Board that the introduction of an
      Ordinance cannot occur at a Special Meeting and he will not be available on January
      23rd. In addition, Director Tobin is not available on that date. The meeting date was not
      changed.

      President Costa requested that Ideas & Suggestions from Board members be added to
      regular Board meeting agendas. The Board Secretary will update the next agenda.


The meeting was adjourned 6:52 p.m.

                           RESOLUTION NO. 20-01

             A RESOLUTION OF THE BOARD OF DIRECTORS
                 OF THE SAN JUAN WATER DISTRICT
                 IN RECOGNITION OF STEVE NUGENT

       WHEREAS, Steve Nugent began his career with Carmichael Water District
in September, 1985; and

      WHEREAS, Mr. Nugent was appointed as General Manager of Carmichael
Water District in July, 2003; and

     WHEREAS, Mr. Nugent retired from Carmichael Water District on
December 27, 2019, concluding a career of 37 years in the water industry; and

      WHEREAS, the San Juan Water District recognizes Mr. Nugent for his
many contributions to the water community, the Sacramento region, the
Carmichael Water District customers and the district’s staff; and

        WHEREAS, the San Juan Water District thanks Mr. Nugent for his
leadership in promoting stewardship of the region’s surface and groundwater
resources and remediation of contamination of the groundwater basin, and his
award-winning work to connect water supplies on both sides of the American River
via a pipeline crossing; and

       WHEREAS, the San Juan Water District also thanks Mr. Nugent for his
commitment to mentoring teens in the Sacramento area, as well as to educating
and training water agency operators and other professionals; and

      WHEREAS, Mr. Nugent will now have more time to enjoy two-wheeled
escapades, perfect his putt, and hang out at his favorite beaches;

      NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the San Juan Water District’s
Board of Directors hereby expresses sincere gratitude and appreciation to Steve
Nugent for his many years of excellent community service as the General
Manager of Carmichael Water District.

        PASSED AND ADOPTED by the Board of Directors of the San Juan Water
District on the 8th day of January 2020.
