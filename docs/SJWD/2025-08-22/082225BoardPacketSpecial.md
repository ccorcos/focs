Subject:      Board Vacancy


RECOMMENDED ACTION
Direct staff regarding filling the vacant seat on the San Juan Water District Board of
Directors.

BACKGROUND
On August 14, 2025, Director Dan Rich notified me and other representatives of the
District that he was resigning from the Board of Directors, effective immediately. The
Board must now determine the process it will use to replace Director Rich. The Board
can do one of three things: 1) appoint a candidate, 2) announce the Board vacancy and
conduct candidate interviews for appointment, or 3) schedule a special election.

Pursuant to Government Code Section 1780, the Board may appoint a successor to
Director Rich no later than 60 days after notice of the Board vacancy was received –
which in this case would mean the deadline for a decision would be October 13.
Government Code section 1780 does not specify a process for the Board to fill a vacancy,
so that process may be structured as the Board chooses. Instead of appointing a
successor to Director Rich, the Board could call a special election to fill the vacancy,
which could occur no earlier than 130 days after the Board’s decision. In 2022 after
Director Hanneman resigned, Sacramento County elections officials estimated that the
cost of holding a special election, just in that county, to be over $1,000,000. Director Rich
represented Division 1, which falls in Placer and Sacramento counties, so a special
election would result in expenses for both counties.

Should the Board not act to either appoint a new Director or schedule a special election
within 60 days of the notice of vacancy (October 13), the decision would then become
the responsibility of the Board of Supervisors of Sacramento County because it is the
county in which the larger portion of the District is located.

In order to be appointed to serve on the Board for this vacancy, applicants must be
registered voters of the District residing in Division 1. If the Board appoints someone to
fill the vacancy, the appointee would hold office until the 2026 District election.
