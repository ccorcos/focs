BACKGROUND
The Regional Water Authority, of which San Juan is a founding member, has two Board
members that represent each member agency – one appointed from the members of the
member agency’s governing body (Board of Directors) and one from the management
team of the agency. One or more alternate RWA Board members may also be appointed
by an RWA member agency, and many RWA member agencies have two or more
alternates. San Juan’s representatives to the RWA Board of Directors are Dan Rich and
Greg Zlotnick, and Ted Costa is the only alternate.
