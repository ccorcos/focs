I.   CALL TO ORDER
     President Rich called the meeting of the San Juan Suburban Water District
     Financing Corporation to order at 6:00 p.m. The Board Secretary took a roll call of
     the Board. The following directors were present: Ted Costa, Marty Hanneman, Ken
     Miller, Dan Rich and Pam Tobin.

     The annual meeting of the Board of Directors of the San Juan Suburban Water
     District Financing Corporation was duly noticed as provided by law to be held at 6:00
     p.m. on December 11, 2019, at the office of the San Juan Water District, 9935
     Auburn Folsom Road, Granite Bay, California.

III.   ACTION ITEMS
       1. Approve minutes of San Juan Suburban Water District Financing
          Corporation Meeting of December 12, 2018

          Director Hanneman moved to approve the minutes of the San Juan
          Suburban Water District Financing Corporation Meeting of December 12,
