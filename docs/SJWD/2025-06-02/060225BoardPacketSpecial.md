                           AMENDMENT NO. 9
             TO AGREEMENT WITH GENERAL MANAGER BETWEEN
                       SAN JUAN WATER DISTRICT
                         AND PAUL HELLIKER


This Amendment No. 9 to the January 11, 2017 agreement between the San Juan Water
District (“District”) and Paul Helliker (“Mr. Helliker”) concerning Mr. Helliker’s employment
as General Manager of the District is made effective as of June 2, 2025, in Granite Bay,
California.

                                       RECITALS:


A.     On January 11, 2017, District and Mr. Helliker entered into an agreement for the
District’s employment of Mr. Helliker as General Manager of the District (“Agreement”);

B.   On June 19, 2021, District and Mr. Helliker amended and restated the Agreement via
Amendment No. 5, to incorporate the previous Amendments;

C.     The District and Mr. Helliker have negotiated and agreed to amend the Agreement to
increase Mr. Helliker’s salary as approved by the Board of Directors on multiple occasions,
including most recently on April 24, 2024, via Amendment No. 8; and

D.     The District and Mr. Helliker have agreed to amend the Amendment to set a mutually
agreed upon termination date for Mr. Helliker’s employment with the District, which will be
March 6, 2026, and is reflected in this Amendment No. 9, which was executed by the Board
in a public meeting on June 2, 2025. While the termination date is March 6, 2026, the Board
intends to immediately commence the recruitment process for a new General Manager. At
the conclusion of the recruitment and upon a new General Manager starting at the District,
Mr. Helliker will serve the District per the Board’s direction until his contract termination
date of March 6, 2026, to support the new General Manager.

                                      AGREEMENT:

1.     Addition of Section 17 to the Agreement. Section 17, as stated below, is hereby added
and incorporated into the Agreement. All other provisions of the Agreement remain in effect.

      a.      Consistent with Section 8a of this Agreement, the parties have mutually
agreed to a termination date for Helliker’s employment with the District. Accordingly, the
