President Costa called the meeting to order at 6:00 p.m.

I.       ROLL CALL
         The Board Secretary took a roll call of the Board. The following directors were present
         in the Boardroom: Ted Costa, Mike McRae, Dan Rich, Pam Tobin and Manuel
         Zamorano.

II.      NEW BUSINESS
         1. General Manager Employment Agreement (W & R)
            Legal Counsel Jones explained that the contract amendment specifies the end of the
            contract as March 6, 2026, and that the recruitment of the General Manager position
            can commence immediately.

            Director Rich moved to approve Amendment No. 9 to the General Manager
            employment agreement. Director McRae seconded the motion and it carried
            unanimously.

      At 6:05 p.m., President Costa announced that the Board was adjourning to Closed Session
      and there were no public comments.
