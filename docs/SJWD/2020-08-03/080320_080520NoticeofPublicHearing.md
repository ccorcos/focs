                            SAN JUAN WATER DISTRICT
                            NOTICE OF PUBLIC HEARING


NOTICE IS HEREBY GIVEN that the Board of Directors of the San Juan Water District
will hold public hearings both in-person at the District Office, 9935 Auburn Folsom Rd,
Granite Bay, CA 95746, and via videoconference in accordance with the Executive Orders
N-29-20 and N-25-20 regarding the COVID-19 emergency, on the following dates:

      Monday, August 3, 2020, at 6:00 p.m., or as soon thereafter as the matter may be
      heard. This public hearing will constitute the first of a series of four public hearings
      the Board of Directors will hold pursuant to Elections Code section 10010 to receive
      public comments regarding the transition of San Juan Water District from at-large
      elections for members of the Board of Directors to by-division elections for members
      of the Board of Directors, the time frame for this transition, the criteria and factors
      that should go into the drawing of maps of single-member voting divisions, the
      composition of the divisions, and other matters related to the election of members of
      the Board of Directors.

      Wednesday, August 5, 2020, at 4:00 p.m., or as soon thereafter as the matter may
      be heard. This public hearing will constitute the second of a series of four public
      hearings the Board of Directors will hold pursuant to Elections Code section 10010
      to receive public comments and input regarding the potential Director election
      division boundaries.

Following the second public hearing, draft maps of potential Board Member election
division boundaries will be published and additional public hearings scheduled.

All interested parties are invited to attend the public hearing and express opinions or
submit information. The public hearings may be continued from time to time, as
necessary, by giving notice at the aforementioned hearing.

The District does not transcribe its proceedings. Persons who wish to obtain a verbatim
record of the proceedings should arrange for attendance by a court reporter. Such
arrangements will be at the sole expense of the person requesting the recordation.
