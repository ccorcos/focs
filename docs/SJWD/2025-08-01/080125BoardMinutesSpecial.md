President Costa called the meeting to order at 2:00 p.m.

I.     ROLL CALL
       The Board Secretary took a roll call of the Board. The following directors were present in
       the Boardroom: Ted Costa, Mike McRae and Manuel Zamorano. Directors Dan Rich and
       Pam Tobin were absent.

II.    DISCUSSION AND ACTION ITEMS
       1. Regular Board Meeting Date for August 2025 (W & R)
          President Costa reported that the August 20, 2025, regular Board meeting was
          previously rescheduled to August 14, 2025, and a resolution is needed to retain the
          meeting as a regular Board meeting.

          Director Zamorano moved to adopt Resolution 25-12 to change the August
          14, 2025, Special Board meeting to a Regular Board meeting. Vice President
          McRae seconded the motion and it carried with 3 Aye votes and 2 Absent
          (Directors Rich and Tobin).

III.   ADJOURN
The meeting was adjourned at 2:02 p.m.
