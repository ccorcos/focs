   Academic Senates’ Reports to the LRCCD Board of Trustees
                          July 2025

District Academic Senate (DAS) President, Paula Cardwell, Reports:
   ●​ Nine members of our District Academic Senate attended the Academic Senate of
       California Community Colleges Leadership Institute June 12-14 in Burlingame!
       We dove deep into the finer points of the Brown Act, parliamentary procedure,
       and myriad other topics. We also had a wonderful opportunity to spend time
       together and build our new team!
   ●​ Our District Academic Senate Fall 25 retreat will take place on Friday, August
       29th starting at 10 am at Howe It’s Done Catering. We are hopeful that the
       schedules of our Board Members will permit representatives to attend!
