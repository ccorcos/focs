Regional Grant Goals
Achieve Hispanic Serving Institution (HSI) status for colleges and universities through the full
postsecondary continuum.

Send 1300 additional young men of color to UC Davis and Sacramento State by 2025 and 2026
respectively.

Triple the number of the region’s students who can graduate from high school with both a diploma
and the freshman year of college completed by 2026.

Triple the number of students who can graduate high school from healthcare and engineering
pathways that articulate to apprenticeships or stacked post secondary certificates and degrees.

Award 2,000 degrees and/or certificates (earned but not claimed), 2,000 degrees and/or
certificates to comebackers, "reverse transfer" 4,000 community college degrees and/or certificate
programs.
