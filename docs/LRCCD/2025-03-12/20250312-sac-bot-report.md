Report provided by: Alisha Nagpal, FLC Student Senate President

Student-led activities (i.e. March in March, campus events, fundraisers):
   ●​ SCC: Continued Student Leadership podcast (two per month). Secretary of Legislative Affairs and SCC
      voting delegate attended SSCCC March in March at the state Capitol on March 4.
   ●​ FLC: Hosted "Falcon’s Got Talent" talent show. Participated in Los Rios annual Lobby Day on March
      11. FLC President and two students attended March in March.
   ●​ CRC: 18 clubs participated in Club Day on February 27. Dance Club performed for Women’s History
      Month on March 4. Student Senate participated in Lobby Day on March 11.

      bylaws.
   ●​ CRC: Appointed students to shared governance committees.
   ●​ ARC: Reviewed Senate and Clubs & Events Board bylaws with governance committees. Appointed six

      committees. Scheduled appointments for Lobby Day on March 11 at the Capitol Swing Space.

Leadership Development Accomplishments (i.e. conferences, trainings, workshops, guest
speakers):

      Center for Online Learning and Teaching. Participated in Lobby Day training with all four colleges,

   ●​ FLC: Participated in Lobby Day training. Approved funding and attendees for General Assembly.
   ●​ CRC: Senate attended the ASACC Conference in Washington, D.C. from March 14-18. Hosted guest
      speaker Andi Adkins Pogue on March 7, discussing course materials and zero textbook costs.
   ●​ ARC: Attended Lobby Day Training at FLC on February 28. Approved funding and attendees for
      General Assembly in Los Angeles (April 4-6).
