Challenges
• OER materials become outdated
  • Curating is time-consuming
• Commercial textbooks provide ancillary
  materials that faculty appreciate and cannot
  recreate the personalized scaffolded
  homework.
• Ensuring disproportionately impacted
  students are enrolling in ZTC/OER courses

ALMC Recommended Goals
• Courses, degrees and certificates that
  have been converted to OER/ZTC will be
  sustained
• Increase ZTC sections by 10% each year
• Within 5 years:
  • 50% of courses have at least one ZTC
     section
  • 50% of degrees and certificates are ZTC

Strategies to Meet Goals
• Full time OER Coordinator
• Reliable means to compensate faculty for
  creating and updating materials
• Resources to purchase class sets for Low
  Textbook Cost (LTC) classes to become ZTC
• Ongoing campaign for faculty and student
  awareness of ZTC
