               Achievements to Date
● Increase in the Percent of Recent high school students enrolling in 15 units
   ○ from 13% in Fall 2018 to 26% in Fall 2023
   ○ even more impactful for our Black students (from 12% to 19%) and Latinx students
      (from 9% to 27%)

● Spring 2024 had the highest success rate since 2010: 72%
● Transfer rate more than doubled since 2016

● Award completion doubled since 2016

● Zero Textbook Cost/Open Educational Resources
   ○ 76% of our course offerings are ZTC in Fall 2024
