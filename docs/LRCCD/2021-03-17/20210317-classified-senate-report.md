AMERICAN RIVER COLLEGE
Olga Prizhbilov, Classified Senate President
The ARC Classified Senate held an appreciation mixer for temp classified employees where
we celebrated our temps and the impact they make on our campus. Thank you to all who
donates their time and finances to support this event.
Nominations for Classified Senate closed on March 12th and we are excited to share the
nominees who will be running for positions in this elections cycle. Look out for our Elections
Newsletter that will be out by the end of this week.
The Senate voted to approve creating 5 scholarships for classified professionals to attend the
Classified Leadership Institute in June. The Religious Inclusion & Observance Taskforce
attended our meeting to share how we can be inclusive of individuals of various religions and
how we can plan campus events and meetings by consulting the religious observance
calendar.

COSUMNES RIVER COLLEGE
Julie Olson, Classified Senate President
On February 11th CRC hosted the 1st part of a four-part series of the Classified Equity
Institute, the feedback from the first session…
   •      CRC had over 150 Classified Professionals participate in Part 1.
   •      Receive feedback from 66 of the participants, through the evaluation.
   •      From the evaluation the common responses we were (somewhat agreed, agreed, or
          strongly agreed)
   •      Classified has better understanding…
          o Why it’s important to focus on racial equity in higher education
          o Better understanding of past and present practices, policies related to college services
              that lead to inequitable outcomes for specific groups of students
          o Have a better understanding of the systemic challenges students face and willing to
              explore potential solutions to address barriers to equity

The 2nd part of Classified Equity Instituted, was held on February 25th. The Agenda for the

   •      Community Working Agreements
   •      Key Takeaways from Classified Institute Part 1
   •      Keynote Speaker- Dr. Cynthia Olivo- The Champion for Change in Centering Antiracism in
          Our Practices
   •      Q & A with Dr. Olivo
   •      Group Activity
   •      Large Group Share out
   •      Conclusion and Evaluation
