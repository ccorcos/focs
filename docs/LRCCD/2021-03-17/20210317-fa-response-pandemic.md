ALLOCATION PHILOSOPHY – INITIAL
Focus on supporting students with financial need
Ensure that our FASFA* and CADAA** students are provided equal treatment
Incentivize students to complete the FASFA and CADAA
Support students taking part-time and full-time loads




*FASFA = Free Application for Federal Student Aid
**CADAA = California Dream Act Application
ALLOCATION PHILOSOPHY – GOING FORWARD
Provide additional support to students taking 15+ units
Provide additional support to students taking transfer-level mathematics and English
Support persistence by providing funding at the start and at the end of the term
Allocate aid over multiple terms to align with the impact of the pandemic
