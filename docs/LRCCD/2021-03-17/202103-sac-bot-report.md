Below is a list of highlighted activities, accomplishments, and events at each of the colleges within the Los Rios District.
This report is to be used as a guide for the Student Advisory Committee representative and the Student Trustee in
presenting the monthly student report at Board of Trustee (BoT) meetings. Only the information on this report will be used.
The Student Trustee, in consultation with the Student Trustee Advisor, will compile these submissions for one
comprehensive report which is due to District Advisor Dr. Rosario, the designated student speaker, and SAC Advisor by
noon the Friday before the BoT meeting. Late submissions/unauthorized reports will not be presented at the BoT meeting.


Student led-activities (i.e. March in March, campus events, fundraisers):
   ● ARC: Donated $2,000 to the Black History Comic Relief event on Friday, Feb 26th. Held a SHPE
       Resume Workshop. Multiple events were held by TRIO STEM. A speaker event was held by the
       Model United Nations Club. Speaker Presentations were hosted by the Progessive Democrats and
       Sociology Club (including one on the impacts of COVID on low-income populations).
   ● CRC: n/a
   ● FLC: Held Club Fair on March 12th and have been actively involved in planning the Region II
       Fundraising Event.
   ● SCC: Held a Virtual Clubs Day with 10 participating clubs, and over 50 students in attendance. They
       are preparing a post-presidential election panel discussion, drafting a support statement to oppose
       Asian American hate speech/behavior, and are drafting a sexual assault awareness campaign.

Senate and Clubs & Events Board Accomplishments (i.e. surveys, engagement in campus and regional
governance):
   ● ARC: Continued work in preparation for a Virtual Club Day to be held on March 18th. They held a
       BoothCentral training, received a financial aid update, and made decisions on SSCCC General
       Assembly voting.
   ● CRC: Continued work on the Diversity Day Event, and made decisions on SSCCC General
       Assembly voting.
   ● FLC: Plans to participate at SSCCC General Assembly. One of the officers is the author of one of the
       statewide resolutions that will be presented at General Assembly. Created an ASFLC website, and
       will be creating an ASFLC Canvas shell.
   ● SCC: Student leaders are participating on the curriculum committee, College Council, Chancellor’s
       Cabinet, hiring committees, SSCCC, and General Assembly.
