CRC Academic Senate President, Scott Crosier, Reports:

   •   On May 14th, during our final senate meeting for the 20-21 year, Claire Oliveros (VPSS) and
       Angela Brady, the CRC Supervisor for Admissions and Records attended. Having both of them
       there provided a unique perspective on the efforts (and need) to reimagine Admissions and
       Records as well as Financial Aid. While VP Oliveros provided a high-level perspective, Ms. Brady
       was able to clarify some of the difficulties faced by our students every day.
   •   A dedicated group of CRC employees organized an impressive and sentimental commencement
       ceremony centered on our students. The drive-thru format gave each CRC employee the
       opportunity to personalize our congratulations to each individual and their friends and family.
   •   Following the commencement ceremony, Dr. Ed Bush invited CRC employees to join him in
       recognizing our concerns, challenges and hopes while he led a candlelight ceremony at our
       central fountain. With the beautiful setting and Ed’s inspiring words, it was a touching and
       memorable moment.
   •   The CRC Academic Senate executive team remains available over the summer.

SCC Academic Senate President, Lori Petite, Reports:

   •   A drive-through Commencement at SCC served as an historic capstone for the Spring 2021
       semester following a largely remote-learning academic year. SCC faculty enthusiastically
       cheered and congratulated hundreds of graduates along the entire commencement route. Our
       students expressed heartfelt appreciation for the opportunity to have their accomplishments
       recognized. While some students drove through by themselves, the vast majority were
       accompanied by friends and family who radiated with pride over their loved one’s
       accomplishment. Many thanks to President Deborah Ortiz and Chancellor King for their
       participation in this year’s event and for making this opportunity for our students possible.
   •   The SCC Academic Senate executive team remains available over the summer.
