4. ACTION

           A.           Public Hearing: Adoption of 2023-24 Proposed Budgets

Public Hearing: Wendy Neilson, Sacramento City College Professor, asked the Board of Trustees
if the replacement of Davies Hall is incorporated in this budget. Executive Vice Chancellor
Rodriguez responded that the short-term and medium-term plans are incorporated. The longer-
term plan will depend on state resources that will all be explored.

A motion was made by Trustee Ortiz, seconded by Trustee Johnson, that the Board of Trustees
adopt the 2023-24 proposed budgets for the General Fund (Z budget), Instructionally-Related,
Special Revenue, Capital Projects, Debt Service, Enterprise, Internal Service, Fiduciary, Trust
and Auxiliary Funds of the District for filing with the appropriate County/State agencies.
