                       LRCCD Board of Trustees Student Report

                                    September Report 9/8/2023

Report provided by: Student Trustee John Doherty

Student-led activities (i.e. March in March, campus events, fundraisers):

   ●  ARC, SCC, and CRC held Welcome Week events.
   ●  SCC and ARC are planning events for Constitution and Voter Registration Day
   ●  FLC held a Club Rush from 9/5-9/7 to bring attention to student clubs.
   ●  ARC is planning an event to promote safety in social settings inside and outside of
      campus.
   ● CRC plans to host a Hawk-O-Ween event in the month of October.
   ● ARC’s Center for Leadership and Development hosted a pancake social for the
      student body to meet their representatives.
Senate and Clubs & Events Board Accomplishments (i.e. surveys, engagement in campus
and regional governance):
          ● All four colleges are successfully filling vacant seats on their student
             government boards
          ● The ARC’s student senate will appoint a student to the hiring committee at
             their next meeting.
          ● FLC’s student government created two committees: a media outreach
             committee and a bylaw committee.
          ● SCC had two of students serving in prominent roles in the Student Senate
             for California Community Colleges.
          ● CRC has reached a total of 17 active clubs on campus.
Leadership Development Accomplishments (i.e. conferences, trainings, workshops, guest
speakers):
   ● CRC, SCC, and FLC have budgeted money for CCCSAA, conference that promotes
      quality student leadership.
   ● SCC and CRC are preparing to attend the SSCCC Advocacy Academy.
   ● ARC’s Club and Events Board is hosting workshops to train current and
      prospective club officers on the basics of club formation and maintenance.
   ● SCC held a training day on the topics of Brown Act and parliamentary procedure.
I have verified the information listed in this report is an accurate account of events in the district.
