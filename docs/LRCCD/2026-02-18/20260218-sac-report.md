                                Student Advisory Committee

              Student Senates’ Reports to the LRCCD Board of Trustees

                                            February 2026

Report presented by Daniya Iralina, Folsom Lake College Student Senate President

 Student-Led Activities:
    •   American River College (ARC) hosted Club Rush on February 4 and will hold its “Love Your
        Community” Club Day on February 24. The Student Senate sponsored the 3rd Annual Iftar on
        February 20 and approved funding to support student transportation and participation for Lobby
        Day on March 10.
    •   Cosumnes River College (CRC) is developing a student-led podcast, and student clubs are
        continuing to host on- and off-campus activities.
    •   Folsom Lake College (FLC) hosted a movie night on February 17 and planned a Casino Night Gala
        for March 13. Another movie night is scheduled for March 24. The Student Senate is organizing a
        pop-up food fundraiser.
    •   Sacramento City College (SCC) welcomed students back with numerous events, including a juried
        art exhibition, Blood Donor Drive, Dress for Success clothing donation drive, Día de Reyes
        celebration, Disability Cultural Center opening, Scholarship Support Sessions, Career Services Open
        House, and an STI Clinic for Sexual Health Week.

 Senate and Clubs & Events Board Accomplishments:
    •   ARC met with campus and district Student Services administrators to strengthen collaboration and
        partnerships supporting students.
    •   CRC’s Legislative Committee is researching bills for Lobby Day. The Senate passed funding for
        Lobby Day and Student Elections, allocated funds to update its office space, and established two ad
        hoc committees focused on blood drives (in partnership with Vitalant) and student housing (in
        collaboration with Hawk Cares).
    •   FLC is organizing Town Halls across its campuses and allocated funds for Town Halls, Lobby Day
        training, and Lobby Day participation. The Senate delegated responsibilities for a food awareness
        initiative and reactivated its Media Outreach Committee to increase event promotion and
        engagement.
    •   SCC appointed two new Senators and continued strengthening student governance operations.
        The Senate funded advocacy programming, professional attire for student leaders engaging in
        lobbying efforts, empowerment grants for clubs, and office improvements. The Joint Budget
        Committee launched a time capsule preservation initiative documenting the years 2018-2025 in
        preparation for the 1927-time capsule opening next year. SCC also secured participation in the
        2026 SSCCC General Assembly and relaunched its student podcast, The Panther’s Post.
 Leadership Development Accomplishments:
     •   ARC approved funding to support staff attendance at the CCCSAA Conference and passed funding
         for SLADE/Lobby Day training to prepare student leaders for advocacy efforts. The Student Senate
         will receive presentations from faculty and campus leaders regarding the ARC Accreditation
         Midterm Report, Learning Resource Center tutoring updates, and the upcoming PTK Honors
         Summit.
     •   CRC will host President Dr. Bush at an upcoming Senate meeting and continues advancing
         academic planning efforts, including increased STEM course offerings for Spring 2026.
     •   FLC’s full Student Senate board attended Spring 2026 Convocation and is developing a student-led
         workshop series. Academic Senate President Wayne Jensen attended the first Student Senate
         meeting of the semester.
     •   SCC’s Secretary of Legislative Affairs hosted advocacy training on reading and understanding
         legislation and federal advocacy preparation. The Student Associated Council will participate in the
         inaugural SSCCC Federal Advocacy Academy in Washington, D.C. and will continue the weekly
         "Super Tuesdays” leadership development series.
