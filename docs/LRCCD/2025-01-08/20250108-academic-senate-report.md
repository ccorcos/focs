   Academic Senates’ Reports to the LRCCD Board of Trustees
                        January 2025

District Academic Senate (DAS) President, Paula Cardwell, Reports:

   ●​ Since our last board meeting, the faculty of Los Rios calculated and posted tens
      of thousands of final grades and are now gearing up and getting our courses
      ready for Spring 25.
   ●​ 2025 will bring major changes to our colleges as Cal-GETC goes into effect in
      the fall and our first Common Course Numbering courses are offered.
   ●​ Our Senate Presidents are busy finalizing our spring hiring committees and we
      are very excited to hire and welcome 75 new faculty in the spring!
   ●​ The District Academic Senate looks forward to a positive and productive Spring
      25 semester
