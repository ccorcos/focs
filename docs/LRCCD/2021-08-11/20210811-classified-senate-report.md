                  LOS RIOS COMMUNITY COLLEGE DISTRICT CLASSIFIED SENATE

                                Board of Trustees Report – August 2021




AMERICAN RIVER COLLEGE
Kristin Farlow, Classified Senate President

The ARC Classified Professionals are beginning Fall Kick Off Weeks (virtually) this week and into
Flex and Convocation week. Classified Professionals have worked hard to prepare campus for our
limited Student Services that will be available on ground and fully available virtually as well as our
limited on-ground courses for Fall semester.

The ARC Classified Senate is hosting a manager supported Classified Meet Up event on August 19th
for all Classified Professionals to meet our new Senate, hear our vision and updates, ask questions,
and get a preview of our upcoming Professional Development opportunities.


COSUMNES RIVER COLLEGE
Tiffany Clark, Classified Senate President

Given recent updates, CRC Student Services leadership and staff are regrouping, planning and
preparing to serve students by appointment only beginning the week of August16th.


FOLSOM LAKE COLLEGE
Andrea Fuentes, Classified Senate President


FLC Classified staff are gearing up for limited in person services. Most departments are set to open
starting Tuesday, August 10th with a limited schedule. In order to ease fears and concerns, the FLC
Classified senate and the Vice President of Administration lead a listening session where updates
were provided from the Director of Operations on new cleaning and safety procedures. Questions
were raised and answered to ensure classified felt heard during this session by our campus
administration. Thank you to our FLC administration for addressing our concerns in this ever-
changing pandemic climate and we look forward to continued collaboration.


SACRAMENTO CITY COLLEGE
Kevin Williams, Classified Senate President

SCC Classified Senate is striving to fill remaining Senator seats for our areas, and have had some
volunteers. We are looking to update the Senate website and provide our Classified professionals
with more up to date information and better clarity into what the Senate does.
