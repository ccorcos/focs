             LOS RIOS COMMUNITY COLLEGE DISTRICT CLASSIFIED SENATE

                             Board of Trustees Report – October 2021

American River College
Kristin Farlow, Classified Senate President

The ARC Classified Senate finished our annual Senate Retreat in September. October has brought much
planning to increase our communication plans, which will include upcoming newsletters, podcasts, and
promotions of ARC Professional Development trainings specifically for Classified Professionals.

Thanks goes to our College President for arranging follow up trainings from our convocation key note
speaker for successful virtual engagement. These will take place in November. We are also in the midst
of technology trainings and offerings for Classified Professionals through our ITC.

The Senate is also planning a virtual social event that will end our Fall Semester to connect and build
community.


Cosumnes River College
Tiffany Clark, Classified Senate President

The CRC Classified Senate received a much-appreciated presentation from Accreditation Leads Emily
Bond and Kim Harrell at our first meeting of the academic year. The presentation consisted of a
summary overview of our ISER and ways for feedback to be shared.

It was decided that we would continue to offer a scholarship sponsored by the Classified Senate at the
new minimum amount of $500. The executive team is currently exploring fundraising ideas to ensure
enough funds are raised to offer the scholarship annually.

The Senate focus for this last month was securing chairs and membership for campus and district
participatory governance committees.

Folsom Lake College
Andrea Fuertes, Classified Senate President

The FLC Classified Senate recently approved the college’s Institutional Self Evaluation Report for our
accreditation process. Additionally, as a senate we have been focused on developing goals for this
academic year and decided to focus on three items: fundraising, collaboration, and engagement. We
hope to create more opportunities to acknowledge our temporary classified staff and build more
collaboration. We also hope to establish a collaborative relationship between our senate and classified
union representatives. Next month we will begin planning for our annual spring classified retreat.
