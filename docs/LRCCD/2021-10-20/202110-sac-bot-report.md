Report provided by ASO Student Senate President/Representative: ARC President Mark Sheppard

Below is a list of highlighted activities, accomplishments and events at each of the colleges within the Los Rios
District. This report is to be used as a guide for the Student Advisory Committee representative and the
Student Trustee in providing the monthly LRCCD student report at the LRCCD Board of Trustee (BOT)
meetings. Only the information on this report will be used. Not all areas need to be completed; rather, this is a
guide on topics to consider. Approved/signed college reports are due to the Student Trustee one week prior to
each scheduled BOT meeting. The Student Trustee in consultation with the SAC Advisor will compile these
submissions for one comprehensive report which is due to District Advisor Dr. Rosario, the designated student
speaker, and SAC Advisor by noon the Friday before the BOT meeting. Late submissions/unauthorized reports
will not be presented at the BOT meeting.

Student led-activities (i.e. March in March, campus events, fundraisers):
   ● ARC: 9/17 was Constitution Day. ASB posted trivia, fun facts and music from Hamilton on its social
       media pages. 9/28 was National Voter Registration Day. ASB encouraged students to register to vote
       via Instagram, Discord and ASB office hours.
   ● CRC: Planning a Giving Tree event with Child Development Center in collaboration with the Basic
       Needs Center for the holidays.
   ● FLC: Participating in marketing campaigns on Canvas pages and virtual classrooms visits.
       Supported the WEAVE advocate Domestic Awareness event
   ● SCC: Secretary of Technology developed student resources hub. Being tested by a few students
       now and will be available to all students soon. Approved new 2021-22 PR budget funded by
       SRF/Fund72 for outreach and advocacy. Forming a stance on the vaccine mandate.

Senate and CAEB Accomplishments (i.e. surveys, engagement in campus and regional governance):
   ● ARC: Created board member bios on Instagram to introduce ASB members. Appointed a Director of
       Communications. Appointed a Vice President for CAEB.
   ● CRC: Elected Student Senate President. Appointed several students to the Student Senate and
       campus committees. Re-chartered 4 clubs. Partnered with the Hawk Cares Program and the Child
       Development Center to bring a Giving Tree and provide gifts for student parents for their children.
   ● FLC: Elected a Senate secretary and two more senators through recruitment efforts. Appointed a
       new Region II delegate. Started a new club.
   ● SCC: CAEB is planning virtual Club Day. Researching the Time Capsules on campus in hopes of
       generating continued student involvement in the future.
Leadership Development Accomplishments (i.e. conferences, trainings, workshops, guest speakers):

   ● CRC: Received the Asian American and Native American Pacific Islander-Serving Institutions
