               LOS RIOS COMMUNITY COLLEGE DISTRICT CLASSIFIED SENATE
                        Board of Trustees Report – December 2020


AMERICAN RIVER COLLEGE
Olga Prizhbilov, Classified Senate President
The ARC Classified Senate hosted our annual Winter Luncheon on Thursday, December 10 th. Over 120
permanent and temporary classified professionals attended. Thank you to ARC management and administrators
for donating over $1,500 in gift cards for our opportunity drawing. The Luncheon was an amazing way to connect,
enjoy our classified staff community, and welcome Melanie Dixon as our new President.
Senator Mario Rodriguez presented a workshop for the California Community Colleges Classified Senate
constituency on Virtual Programming and how to stay connected in a remote environment.

COSUMNES RIVER COLLEGE
Julie Olson, Classified Senate President
CRC Classified Senate will be hosting a zoom etiquette workshop before the semester ends, for all Classified to
attend. The open forums with President Bush, and his executive team will continue the Spring semester.



FOLSOM LAKE COLLEGE
Lindsey Campbell, Classified Senate President
FLC Classified have been hard at work assisting in the completion of the draft Institutional Self Evaluation Report
(ISER) for our future Accreditation visit. Last month the Career and Transfer Center launched their new career
assessment tool, PathwayU. The Classified Professional Development Committee also held a listening session in
November to gather concerns and recommendations regarding working remotely to see how the professional
development committee can assist their classified colleagues.

SACRAMENTO CITY COLLEGE
Ahmad Sear Ameerzada, Classified Senate President
Sacramento City College conducted Classified Senate Elections for the Secretary vacant position. Election results
will be finalized on December 14th.

The SCC Classified Senate in a group effort with the VPA Office will purchase and distribute How to be an
Antiracist? Book to the Classified Professionals campus wide.
