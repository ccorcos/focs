LRCFT Report to the Los Rios Board of Trustees    August 9, 2023
On behalf of the LRCFT, I want to thank the trustees and
chancellor for agreeing to a 12% Retro and 8% COLA. Faculty
are quite happy with these improvements, including the
expansion of parental leave and part time faculty health
insurance.
At the start of Fall semester, LRCFT will provide several
training sessions on our new contract provisions, including chair
compensation, and parental and catastrophic leaves.
Next week, LRCFT will join the Academic Senate and District
for a welcome event to honor the new full time faculty hires.
In giving back to our membership, the LRCFT will once again
be a major sponsor of the SCLC annual Labor Day picnic. We
will also conduct a Membership drive during this academic year.
Local political races are heating up. The LRCFT PAFC recently
interviewed 7 candidates for AD 6. Our PAFC voted to endorse
Paula Villescaz. We will also hold interviews for AD 7,
Sacramento mayor, city council, and supervisor races.
The LRCFT looks forward to continuing our partnership with
LRCCD in this new academic year.


Thank you!
Jason Newman LRCFT President
