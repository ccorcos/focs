SolarShares Program
 Program started in 2018

 Fixed contract amount is 28,379,532 kWh/year for 20 years

 Equates to approximately 65% of total District-wide green
  electricity usage in kWh

 Considering total energy both (electricity and gas) this
  percentage equates to approximately 40% carbon free green
  energy

 SolarShares Program created a reduction in Green House Gas
  (GHG) emissions equivalent to 8,075 metric tons of carbon

    Reduce exposure to hazardous chemicals

    Improve indoor air quality

 Nanoseptic technology
    NanoSeptic technology kills pathogens using a photocatalytic reaction
       with embedded titanium dioxide nanoparticles. In plain language, it
       can provide a self-disinfecting surface for high traffic areas by using UV
       light. The technology uses a septic coating on self-adhesive plastic
       sheets that self-clean every time they're touched.
 100% implementation by start of 2022-’23school year
