LRCCD Classified Senate Board Meeting – August 2022 Report



Folsom Lake College

Karla Lozano, Classified Senate President

In collaboration with the Classified Professional Development committee, Classified Senate, and the
FLC Vice President of Administration, the New Employee Orientation Event took place on August 3rd,
2022. The goal is to hold this event each semester to welcome new employees to campus. The Caring
Campus team is planning for the “First Week Welcome” on ground to be present for students. In
addition, the team will be presenting Caring Campus during Flex Week. Many Classified staff members
will be presenting other Flex week sessions (some in collaboration with faculty and
administration), including presentations related to participatory governance, financial aid, supportive
services, and student support. Classified staff are in full force preparing for fall.



All other campuses, nothing to report at this time.
