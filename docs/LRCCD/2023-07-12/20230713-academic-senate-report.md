                Academic Senates’ Reports to the LRCCD Board of Trustees
                                      July 2023


District Academic Senate (DAS) President, Alisa Shubb, Reports:

On July 3, 2023, the District Academic Senate President was informed about the proposal to
purchase a Salesforce Customer Relations Management (CRM) tool. Since District Academic
Senate meetings are not held in the summer when faculty are off contract, the DAS itself was
effectively not informed, nor were we able to share any information about, discuss, or
contribute to this decision. At this time, the DAS is unable to determine whether or not the
proposed uses for the Salesforce CRM are directly related to any of the 10+1 academic and
professional matters. DAS leadership is aware of many potential uses of a CRM that would fall
under faculty purview, and we do expect to engage in collegial consultation on any of those
future uses.
