Presented by Student Advisory Committee member - CRC Student Senate President Zain Ali
Below is a list of highlighted activities, accomplishments, and events at each of the colleges within the Los Rios District.
This report is to be used as a guide for the Student Advisory Committee representative and the Student Trustee in
presenting the monthly student report at Board of Trustee (BoT) meetings. Only the information on this report will be used.
The Student Trustee, in consultation with the Student Trustee Advisor, will compile these submissions for one
comprehensive report which is due to District Advisor Dr. Rosario, the designated student speaker, and SAC Advisor by
noon the Friday before the BoT meeting. Late submissions/unauthorized reports will not be presented at the BoT meeting.
Student led-activities (i.e. March in March, campus events, fundraisers):
   ● ARC: Represented Ethiopia at the virtual Model United Nation Far West summit: chosen to be a
       plenary speaker and received three awards. TRIO held a successful “Art to Heart” event, complete
       with music, and art kits led by Student Senator Marykay Maduike.
   ● CRC: Had Student elections. Oliver J. Durand Scholarship of $2,500 awarded exemplifying
       leadership. Four Outstanding Contribution and Service Awards worth $150 each for leadership.
   ● FLC: Events held by the Clubs and Events Board. Volunteering in the commencement ceremony.
   ● SCC: DeStress Fest: 140 students to participate in a 4-day pre-finals event: painting/making
       boba/yoga/mediation. Native American Land Acknowledgement statement in progress.
Senate and Clubs & Events Board Accomplishments (i.e. surveys, engagement in campus and regional
governance):
   ● ARC: Stacey Burrows elected Senate President: Mark Sheppard re-elected as CAEB President. Pet
       Palooza was successful: tour of Sacramento SPCA, introduced to dogs, cats, and small animals
       available for adoption. Two Dungeons & Dragons virtual practice sessions given to students.
   ● FLC: Actively participated in the Accreditation Committee. Planning meeting scheduled for summer.
   ● SCC: Student leaders meeting with campus committee to develop new SCC vision statement. Live
       Streamed “Get Out the Vote” event. Student Senate president spoke on the importance of voting.
Leadership Development Accomplishments (i.e. conferences, trainings, workshops, guest speakers):
   ● FLC: Welcomed the new student leaders: conducted last student senate meeting
   ● SCC: PRIE staff and former SCC student (now CRCF counselor) spoke to student leaders on
       demographics of constituency, delegating, and legacy. Participating in the transition process for fall.

I have verified the information listed in this report is an accurate account of events in the district.
