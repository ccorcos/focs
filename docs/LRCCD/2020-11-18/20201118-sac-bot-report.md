Below is a list of highlighted activities, accomplishments and events at each of the colleges within the Los Rios
District. This report is to be used as a guide for the Student Advisory Committee representative and the
Student Trustee in providing the monthly LRCCD student report at the LRCCD Board of Trustee (BOT)
meetings. Only the information on this report will be used. Not all areas need to be completed; rather, this is a
guide on topics to consider. Approved/signed college reports are due to the Student Trustee one week prior to
each scheduled BOT meeting. The Student Trustee in consultation with the SAC Advisor will compile these
submissions for one comprehensive report which is due to District Advisor Dr. Rosario, the designated student
speaker, and SAC Advisor by noon the Friday before the BOT meeting. Late submissions/unauthorized reports
will not be presented at the BOT meeting.
 Student led-activities (i.e. March in March, campus events, fundraisers):
      ● ARC: ARC compiled questions and held a Student Impressions Panel with the new ARC President
           and Vice President of Instruction on November 13th, 2020.
      ● CRC: CRC is working to amend their constitution and bylaws, create a Transcript Notation
           Resolution, provide Stress Relief events, do Block Grant Advocacy, conduct Lobby Day bill
           research, and create a Canvas course to further outreach and connection to students.
      ● FLC: FLC held a Halloween Costume Contest, virtual Club Day with 35 students and 6 clubs
           present, virtual Falcon’s Day Event, SSCCC Town Hall, and is planning a Student Recognition Event.
      ● SCC: SCC held a Post Presidential Election Forum, and is planning a virtual tour of the laboratory
           focusing on STEM occupations.

 Senate and CAEB Accomplishments (i.e. surveys, engagement in campus and regional governance):
    ● ARC: ARC launched a new YouTube channel, held a virtual club day, and provided resources to
        students on California propositions.
    ● CRC: CRC held a Student Forum with the VP of Student Services on October 29th, conducted
        voting promotional activities, passed a resolution in support of Proposition 16, and hosted a
        successful virtual Hawk-O-Ween event.
    ● FLC: FLC created a draft for a ASFLC Website, appointed POIs to all newly formed districtwide
        student workgroups, and has appointed representatives to all PG Committees.
    ● SCC: SCC participated in the Ballot Bowl registering nearly 400 new voters, and held a Mental
        Health Campaign in partnership with the campus Health Center.
