            LOS RIOS COMMUNITY COLLEGE DISTRICT CLASSIFIED SENATE

                            Board of Trustees Report – July 2021

AMERICAN RIVER COLLEGE
Kristin Farlow, Classified Senate President

       The ARC Classified Senate gathered for a Celebration and “change of office” for the
       outgoing and incoming Senate, co-hosted by our Vice President of Administrative
       Services and the Classified Senate. ARC’s two-term President, Olga Prizhbilov vacated
       her office two weeks early after accepting her new managerial position at District Office
       as Director of Refugee Career Pathways – Congratulations, Olga!

       ARC has several Officers and Senators serving in new positions or for the first time ever
       this election cycle. Service began July 1, 2021 for this term. Due to promotions accepted
       elsewhere, many appointments were made by the President post-election. We currently
       have 19 out of 20 positions filled. The Past-President is our final vacancy to appoint in
       the near future. New Officers and Senators are listed below:

       President: Kristin Farlow
       Vice President: Cesar Reyes
       Treasurer: Tatyana Torgashev
       Communications Officer: Liz Geisser
       Senator at Large: Israeline Grayson

       Senator, Operations/IT: Corey Winfield
       Senator, Administration: Nimo Ali
       Senator, Outreach/Workforce: Amber Common
       Senator, Instruction: Kevin Porter

       Senator, Student Services: Robert Smith
       Senator, Student Services: Fleurdeliza Lipscomb
       The Senate is planning a virtual “Speed Staffing” event for all Classified Professionals in
       early August. The purpose is to meet the new Senate leadership and each other with so
       many transitions and interim positions having shifted over this past year.
