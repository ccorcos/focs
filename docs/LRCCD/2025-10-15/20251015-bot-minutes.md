       A.      Chancellor Search Update: Job Posting and Screening Committee

meetings, and future meetings once applicants are screened.

7. BOARD MEMBER REPORTS

Trustee Nelson toured Sacramento City College with her high school and attended the Diwali
Festival in Elk Grove.

Trustee Pham reported on the process of appointing students to district committees. The student
advisory committee received informative presentations from Mark MacDonald, Gabe Ross, and
Paula Cardwell.

Trustee Johnson attended the American River College Career Technical Education building grand
opening event.

Trustee Harris-Mathews acknowledged Hispanic Heritage month and all the welcoming events
and activities happening at our colleges. She also reported on other activities, including the
Rainbow Chamber dinner and events at the campuses.

Trustee Wilkerson thanked Peter Khang for his service as Interim Chief Counsel. She attended
the Excellence in Education Awards and other community events in Yolo County, as well as
Sacramento City College’s 10th anniversary of HSI status celebration.
