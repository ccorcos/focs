  Enhanced Air Traffic Control (AT-CTI)
Special Student Requirements
     ○   US Citizen

     ○   Speaks English Clearly (Level 4 -Operational Proficiency
         Standard/ICAO Doc 9835)
     ○   Must be under 31-years old by tentative job offer after graduation.
     ○   Have one year of general work experience, or four years of education
         leading to a Bachelor's degree, or any combination of education and
         experience equaling one year of general experience.

     ○   Security Clearance (Tier 3) for AT-CTI & (Tier 2) Contract Tower
          ■ Extensive background check.
     ○   Class 3 Airman Medical Exam to enter the program and a FAA
         endorsed medical and psychological examination upon FAA
         tentative offer for placement at facility.

     Enhanced Air Traffic Control (AT-CTI)
Enhanced Program Overview
 ●   16 Students per Cohort
            ■   8 Students in Tower Specialty / 8 in En Route Specialty

 ●   Program & Milestones
      ○ 3 Semesters (GE must be completed before starting cohort)

      ○   Pre-Program - Introductory / Priority Enrollment Course

      ○   Air Traffic Skills Assessment (ATSA) Results (Aptitude)
            ■ Best & Well Qualified Scores.

      ○   3rd Semester is entirely Simulation / Scenario Training
            ■ Student/Instructor Ratio 1:1 (50 hours)

      ○   Final Evaluations
            ■ 4 - 30min evals over the course of 72hrs with a cumulative score
                of 70% or better.

      ○   Student receives SCC endorsement and applies through USA Jobs.
