2. ORAL COMMUNICATIONS
The public may comment on any items identified on the agenda for this special meeting of the Board of Trustees. The law prohibits
action by the Board on non-agenda items. Speakers are limited to three minutes. If you wish to speak on a particular item on this
special meeting agenda, your comments will be taken up at the time the Board takes up that item.

Members of the public have two options to offer public comment:
1) Use the Chat function in Zoom to submit your full name and the matter you wish to speak about to the Clerk of the Board. You
   will be called on by the Board President during this portion of the meeting.
2) Use the Hand Raising Function in Zoom to indicate you’d like to speak during the public comments portion of the meeting. If
   participating by phone, you may “raise your hand” by dialing *9. When you raise your hand, you will be recognized by the
   President of the Board and asked to provide your full name and the matter you wish to speak about.


3. PARTICIPATORY GOVERNANCE WORKSHOP (COLLEGIALITY IN ACTION)

League of California will facilitate this informational session and discussion related to collegial consultation
and decision-making for the LRCCD Board of Trustees, District administrators, and the District Academic
Senate.
