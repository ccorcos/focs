LOS RIOS COMMUNITY COLLEGE DISTRICT CLASSIFIED SENATE
Board of Trustees Report – November 2021

American River College
Kristin Farlow, Classified Senate President

The ARC Classified Senate has been reviewing the ARC ISER this month for feedback. We are thankful for
continued updates and communication from our VPA at Senate meetings concerning all areas of
campus.

Senate hosted a listening session for our VPA to follow up on her hiring/impression panel promise for
hearing and valuing the needs and requests of Classified Professionals for the areas she oversees. We
are continuing to collect and summarize ideas and issues for IT, Operations, and Business Services at
ARC for improvements and changes.

We continue to promote current professional development opportunities specifically designed for
Classified Professionals scheduled out for Fall. Senate will begin discussing our Annual Spring PD Day;
this is sponsored by ARC Classified Senate for all Classified Professionals. Historically, we have met off
campus/in-person and applied for funding from LRCEA for support. Decisions on in-person versus virtual
offerings will be made as we continue to learn more about Spring reopening and collect feedback via
surveying our constituents.


Cosumnes River College
Tiffany Clark, Classified Senate President

Nothing to report at this time.

Folsom Lake College
Andrea Fuertes, Classified Senate President

The FLC Classified Senate is working on their top three goals for this academic year including building a
stronger collaborative relationship with the classified union representatives at our campus. We are also
building stronger professional development opportunities for our classified staff and starting to plan for
our annual spring retreat.

Additionally, we recognize as a senate the need to connect and celebrate our temporary classified staff
members and are working on a celebration that will take place Spring 2022.
