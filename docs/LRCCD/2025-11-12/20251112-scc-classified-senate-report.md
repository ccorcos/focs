SCC Classified Senate Reports - Trustee Board Meeting

November 2025

At Sacramento City College, our Classified Senate has been busy continuing to build engagement,
connection, and care across our campus community. Recently, we’ve worked collaboratively to
support and rank two new full-time Classified positions, creating a classified workgroup with
representation from across all three college service areas to develop a rubric that ties directly to
our college’s vision, values, and strategic plan — ensuring our process is transparent and aligned
with our campus priorities. We look forward to welcoming these new positions and more to come
as our student numbers continue to grow.
We’re also in the process of updating our Senate bylaws to make them more inclusive and
strengthen participation from our Classified Professionals, with allowances for classified senators
to share positions, expansion of our cabinet to include Senator-at-Large positions, and increasingly
inclusive language towards Classified Temporary employees.
We are thrilled to have a dedicated, robust, group of Classified leaders who have recently created
monthly restorative communal practices in our Rooted Sessions, and opportunities to connect
cross-divisionally during our Coffee & Tea Time hours.
Looking ahead, we’re very excited about a couple of upcoming events — our Winter Cheer Lunch &
Learn on December 18th, where we’ll gather for a potluck and conversation with President Albert
Garcia on campus planning systems, and our Classified Professional Learning Day in January,
which will focus on growth, collaboration, and care.
As always, our goal is to continue uplifting Classified voices and creating spaces where everyone
feels valued and connected to the mission of SCC.
