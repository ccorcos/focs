                             Student Advisory Committee

                            Associated Student Body Report

                                           May 2025


Report provided by: Bridget O’Neil, SCC Student Senate President

Student-led activities (i.e. March in March, campus events, fundraisers):
   ●​ SCC: Hosted Get Out The Vote days on May 22–23, with cafe vouchers for student voters.
      Planned leadership recognition ceremony and Garden of Dreams networking/advocacy
      event.
   ●​ FLC: Held a movie day showing Cars. Preparing for the Student Awards Gala on May 24 to
      honor club and student government involvement.
   ●​ CRC: Multiple clubs engaged in off-campus activities: Outdoors Club visited Cronan Ranch,
      Psychology Scholars visited UC Davis, Photography Club toured Crocker Art Museum, and
      Inclusive Nutrition Club visited Better Meat Co.
   ●​ ARC: Planning an end-of-year retreat and transition meeting for current and incoming ASB
      members (June 3–4 at Folsom Courtyard Marriott).

Senate and Clubs & Events Board Accomplishments (i.e. surveys, engagement in campus
and regional governance):
   ●​ SCC: Student Senate President and Secretary of Public Relations testified at a Higher
      Education Committee hearing in support of AB 409 (teleconferencing for student
      organizations). Also tabled at Celebrate City event.
   ●​ FLC: Completed 2025–26 elections. Identified Senate vacancies (Director of Legislative
      Affairs, Secretary, Senators) and CAEB vacancies (Vice Commissioner, Secretary, Finance
      Director, Historian, Delegates). Approved recommendation to adopt the 2025–26 College
      Strategic Plan. Participated in Chancellor search forum on May 7.
   ●​ CRC: Passed funding for Rock the Vote events. Senate and CAEB participated in the
      Chancellor Forum.
   ●​ ARC: Passed Resolution S25-01 advocating for student rights amid national uncertainty.
      Held elections with food and voter engagement at ARC and Natomas Center. Psi Beta club
      conducted a transportation survey and will present findings on May 15.

Leadership Development Accomplishments (i.e. conferences, trainings, workshops, guest
speakers):
   ●​ SCC: Attended General Assembly. Hosted guest speakers President Albert Garcia and
      Director Camille Broussard Wise. Held a 5th Tuesday scavenger hunt-style training.
   ●​ FLC: Transferring ASFLC class participated in a college-wide recognition ceremony.
   ●​ CRC: Welcomed President Bush to the Senate meeting on April 25 for institutional updates.
   ●​ ARC: Approved preliminary funding for CCCSAA 2025 conference (Santa Clara, October
      17–19). Appointed students to 12 faculty hiring committees, VPSS panel, and had 9
      students attend Chancellor feedback session with PPL Consulting on May 5.
