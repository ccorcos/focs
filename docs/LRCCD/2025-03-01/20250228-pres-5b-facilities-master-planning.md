The Facilities Master Plan (FMP) Process
 Strategic Vision
    Strategic Level Planning Effort for Enhancing Facilities
    Supports and Reflects the Educational Plan and Vision
     of each College
 Space Optimization
 Prioritization of Projects
 Sustainability Goals
 Student, Faculty and Staff Engagement
 Sets Standards and Expectations
 Financial Planning
 Improves and Enhances the College Experience

The FMP Process
Key Components:
 Assessment of existing facilities
 Identification of gaps and areas for improvement
 Stakeholder engagement (students, faculty, staff)
 Proposed Enhancements to Facilities
    Upgrading classrooms, lecture halls and labs
    Creating collaborative spaces (study lounges,
     group work areas)
    Expanding technology resources (computer labs,
     online access)
