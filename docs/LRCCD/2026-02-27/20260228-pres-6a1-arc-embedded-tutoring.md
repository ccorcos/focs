What is Embedded Tutoring
The Embedded Tutoring Program places trained student
tutors directly into speciﬁc course sections, particularly in
those with high D, F, or withdrawal grades and
equity-priority disciplines such as English, Math,
Chemistry, and Biology.

Embedded tutors:
  ● Attend class sessions
  ● Collaborate with faculty
  ● Provide course-aligned academic support

This model brings tutoring to students early and reduces
barriers to access.
In-Person & Online
Embedded tutoring is oﬀered for both in-person and
online courses:

● In-Person Classes: The embedded tutor attends
  every class session alongside students. This helps
  the tutor understand the course material, the
  instructor’s teaching style, and student needs.

● Online Classes: The embedded tutor is added to
  the course in Canvas as a tutor, allowing them to
  access course materials and stay up to date with
  assignments, deadlines, and discussions.

2024 to 2025 Results
● Embedded tutoring participation was
  associated with signiﬁcantly higher course
  success rates, 75% compared to 58%, a
  17-percentage point advantage
● Embedded tutoring participants also saw an
  improvement of almost three-quarters of a
  letter grade (2.62 vs 1.89 GPA).
● No disproportionate impact: First-generation,
  low-income, and student of color are
  overrepresented in participation and thriving.
