                                  Student Advisory Committee

                                Associated Student Body Report

                                                 April 2025


Report provided by: Miranda Roccucci, CRC Student Senate President

Student-led activities (i.e. March in March, campus events, fundraisers):
   ●​ SCC: Continued Student Leadership podcast and began planning for Student Elections.
   ●​ FLC: Hosted a movie night on March 26 featuring Puss in Boots and a Casino Night on March 28.
   ●​ CRC: CAEB planning Stress Relief Days. The Women’s Rights and Activism Club supported Women’s
      History Month events.
   ●​ ARC: ARC Rugby Club won the D1AA NorCal Finals and will play University of San Diego for the state
      championship on April 5. Held Fem Fest on March 26 with guest speakers including President Lisa
      Cardoza. Hosted the Second Annual Iftar Celebration on March 27. Model UN Club preparing for the
      Far West Conference (April 11–15).

Senate and Clubs & Events Board Accomplishments (i.e. surveys, engagement in campus
and regional governance):
   ●​ SCC: Installed a water filter in the student leadership office. Created flyers about free water bottles.
      Finished SAC banner. Secretary of Legislative Affairs made public comment supporting three
      California Assembly Bills (AB 323, AB 363, AB 335).
   ●​ FLC: Reviewed student senate survey results and appointed a new senator.
   ●​ CRC: Updated governing documents for both boards. Elected Ethan Bui as SSCCC Delegate.
   ●​ ARC: 21 students petitioned to run in 2025–26 ASB elections. Hosting ARC and Los Rios Student
      Trustee Candidate Forum on April 15. 42 clubs chartered by CAEB. Updated CAEB By-Laws to be
      voted on during elections. Planning April 22 Club Day to coincide with elections.

Leadership Development Accomplishments (i.e. conferences, trainings, workshops, guest
speakers):
   ●​ SCC: Participated in Lobby Day. Guest speaker Delrio Mercier discussed an E-Waste program. Full
      council reviewed over 70 SSCCC resolutions in preparation for General Assembly.
   ●​ FLC: Students attended SSCCC General Assembly.
   ●​ CRC: Attended Lobby Day (March 11) and ASACC Conference in Washington D.C. (March 14–18).
   ●​ ARC: Students trained for faculty hiring committees by Interim VP of Student Services, with over 15
      students attending. Will attend General Assembly virtually (April 4–6). Attended ASACC Conference
      in Washington D.C.
