6. BOARD MEMBER REPORTS

Trustee Sougrati reported out from recent meetings the student leaders have participated in.

Trustee Nelson acknowledged that she’s seeing a lot of students announcing that they’re going
to CRC, and it’s exciting to see.

Trustee Knight thanked President Pimentel for his recent presentations on the California
community college system and Los Rios at local rotary meetings?

Trustee Ortiz expressed her concerns about the impact that current federal practices with
immigration and deportation are having locally and potentially on our students.

Trustee Harris-Mathews followed up on Trustee Ortiz’s remarks and noted that she is leveraging
her representation on national associations such as ACCT to stay informed and engaged on these
issues on behalf of the Board from a policy perspective.

Trustee Wilkerson added to the immigration and deportation discussion, noting that the
district’s budget includes resources to help our students who need support. She also thanked
the Board’s Chancellor Seach Ad Hoc Committee members for their work to screen the
proposals…
