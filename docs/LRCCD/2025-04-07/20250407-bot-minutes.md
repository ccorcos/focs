1. CALL TO ORDER

The special board meeting was called to order by President Wilkerson at 5:30 pm, in the Board
Room at 1919 Spanos Court, Sacramento, CA 95825.

   Colette Harris-Mathews

2. ORAL COMMUNICATIONS

There were no public comments.

3. INFORMATION

       A.      Interview of Finalists for Chancellor’s Search Consultant

Interim Chief Counsel Peter Khang provided information on the Board’s task in interviewing the
finalists for the Chancellor’s search consultant.

4. ACTION

       A. Selection of Chancellor’s Search Consultant

The Board of Trustees interviewed finalists (AGB, CCCS, and PPL) to be considered as the
Chancellor’s Search Consultant. This action item followed the interview of finalists in open
session.

A motion was made by Trustee Ortiz, seconded by Trustee Knight, that the Board of Trustees
approve the hiring of PPL to facilitate the Chancellor search process and directed staff to schedule
a Special Board Meeting on April 9 to approve the contract.

Roll Call Vote:
Aye: Harris-Mathews, Johnson, Jones, Knight, Ortiz, Nelson, Wilkerson
No: None
Absent: None
