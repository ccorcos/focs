3. BOARD STUDY SESSION

   A. Increasing Student Access through Equity-Informed Strategic Enrollment Management

The four Los Rios College Presidents, Melanie Dixon (American River College), Edward Bush
(Cosumnes River College), Whitney Yamamura (Folsom Lake College), Michael Gutierrez
(Sacramento City College) and Deputy Chancellor Nye presented a college-specific and
districtwide overview of enrollment trends including:

   •   Los Rios enrollment trends for the past ten years and regional, statewide, and national
       trends in community college enrollment.
   •   The current state of dual enrollment at Los Rios and examination of districtwide and
       regional potential and opportunities.
   •   The current state of online education at Los Rios and examination of student demand in
       the region, statewide and nationally.
