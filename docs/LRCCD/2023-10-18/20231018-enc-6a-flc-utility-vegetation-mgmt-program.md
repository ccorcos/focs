Performance Measures
Certificate Requirements
1. Tree Care Industry Association - Electrical Hazards Awareness
   Program (TCIA EHAP): must pass with 80% or better

1. Technical Studies: must pass the final exam with 80% or better.

3. Tree ID/Map Reading: must pass a field test with 80% or better
    • find predetermined locations
    • correctly ID the tree species

4. Pre-Inspection of a Power Line:
    • locate, inspect and accurately record data in an assigned
      area
    • record at least twenty-five trees that are 12 feet or closer to the
      proximity of a high voltage line.
