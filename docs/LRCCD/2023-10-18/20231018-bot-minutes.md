       A. Folsom Lake College: Utility Vegetation Management Workforce Training Programs

Folsom Lake College staff presented an overview of the Utility Vegetation Management
Workforce Training Programs, which, in partnership with Butte College, is part of a collaborative
of more than 30 California Community Colleges that make up the Upskill California consortium
where we are committed to delivering customized employee training and workforce training. The
Utility Vegetation Management Workforce Training Programs provide trainees with rigorous,
industry leading training that prepares them for direct entry into an arborist career.
