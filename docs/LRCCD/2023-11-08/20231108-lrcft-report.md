LRCFT Report to the Los Rios Board of Trustees   Nov. 8, 2023
As part of our membership appreciation drive, our membership
two weeks ago attended a local theatrical performance at the B
St. theater. “Papi, Me, and Cesar Chavez.”
We will also hold a membership appreciation holiday party on
December 2 at Track 7 Brewery in Curtis Park.
The LRCFT participated and contributed to West Sacramento’s
Dia de los Muertos event on November 5.
The LRCFT contributed to and will participate in the
Democratic Party’s Tower Bridge Awards next week.
Along with Trustee Kelly Wilkerson and the CTA, the LRCFT
participated in the recall campaign of Emily McDonald, a
recently elected trustee in Woodland Joint Unified School
District. McDonald has expressed in open board meetings a
litany of hateful statements against LGBTQ students.
The LRCFT PAFC over the next two months will interview
candidates for the Sacramento City Council races. We will also
phonebank for our endorsed candidate, Paula Villescaz, running
for Assembly district 6.
The LRCFT will hold elections in November and December for
the college president position at several of the campuses.
The LRCFT is taking an active role in helping to sponsor the
upcoming The March in March student march and rally at the
state capitol on March 7. The CFT is a major sponsor of the
event.
We recently met with our SCTA union counterparts to assist
LRCCD in reaching a final agreement with Sac Unified
regarding dual enrollment.
We want to thank Trustee Deborah Ortiz for attending our recent
LRCFT board meeting and discussing the role of trustees in the
district.
The LRCFT recently finalized an agreement with LRCCD to
increase compensation for part time faculty professional
development. We want to thank the district for partnering with
us. This is a path-breaking agreement that is rare among districts
in the state.
The LRCFT will meet with the District in the next two weeks to
negotiate faculty compensation for the Davies Hall closure and
Moratorium on the use of human remains.
In closing, the LRCFT participated in the AVC of Finance
hiring committee. We congratulate Jaime Ruggles.
Thank you!
Jason Newman
LRCFT President
