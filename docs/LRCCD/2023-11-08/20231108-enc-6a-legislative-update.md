Budget Act - COLA

● The budget uses about $290 million of one-time funds to support a
  $678 million investment in apportionment costs for 2023-24.


● This means that approximately 42% of the COLA is allocated using
  one-time funding creating a structural deficit that will need to be
  filled with the 2024-25 budget.


● Next year’s COLA is initially estimated to be approximately 3%. The
  budget will have to backfill the $290 million and fund the 3%.

Issues for 2024

● COLA & Base Increase


  Ø COLA estimated at this point to be about 4%.

  Ø Top priority will be to backfill one-time funding going to COLA in 2023-24 and
    fund the additional COLA in 2024-25.

Issues for 2024

● Funding Formula


  Ø Funding floor kicks in for community colleges in 2025-26 so this is the last year
     that those districts that are not on the formula will receive a COLA.


  Ø Discussions regarding impact of formula on districts that are either on the
     funding floor or are on ECA.


  Ø How much growth ought to be funded and how should it be applied.

● Implementation of Cal Grant Equity Framework


  Ø The framework is in statute, it needs to be properly funded.

  Ø Would create a Cal Grant 2 for community colleges and a Cal Grant 4 for four-
