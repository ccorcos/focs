   Academic Senates’ Reports to the LRCCD Board of Trustees
                        August 2025

District Academic Senate (DAS) President, Paula Cardwell, Reports:

   ●​ The District Academic Senate will be teaming up with Vice Chancellor Carrie
      Bray and our Union leadership to welcome dozens of new faculty to our district
      this coming Tuesday at American River College. We are very excited to warmly
      welcome our new faculty!
   ●​ Our District Academic Senate Fall Retreat will take place on Friday, August 29th
      from 10 am to 1 pm at Howe It’s Done Catering. We will be setting goals relating
      to finalizing our proposed NAGPRA policy, next steps in the exploration of
      Noncredit Instruction, and the revision of our Hiring Manual. The District Equity
      and Student Success Committee will also be working on AI Guidance and
      Policies this year.
   ●​ We welcome back our incredible faculty and wish them an absolutely amazing
      Fall 25 semester.

Cosumnes River College Senate President, Lauren Wagner, Reports:
  ●​ CRC is excited to welcome our new and returning faculty to the new academic
     year.
  ●​ We are looking forward to a robust set of Professional Development offerings

  ●​ The Student Equity Plan adhoc committee met on August 5th to share findings of

Folsom Lake College Senate President, Wayne Jensen, Reports
   ●​ Faculty are excited for the upcoming academic year with increased enrollments,
      a beautiful new science building, and wonderful new tenure-track faculty who will
      help us in our continued commitment to equitable outcomes.
   ●​ We look forward to working with other constituency leaders to finalize our
      Facilities Master Plan and Student Equity and Achievement Plan during this
      upcoming Fall 25 semester.
   ●​ We hope you will be able to join us a week from today on Wednesday, August
      20th for the grand opening of our new Oak Hall science building.
