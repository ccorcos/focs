2. ORAL COMMUNICATIONS
The public may comment on any items within the Board’s jurisdiction, even if the items are not on the agenda only during this
portion of the meeting. However, the law prohibits action by the Board on non-agenda items. Speakers are limited to three minutes.

Members of the public who wish to provide public comment will be able to raise their hand on the Zoom application. If you are
calling in on a phone connection, you may raise your hand by dialing *9. When you raise your hand, you will be recognized by the
President of the Board and asked to provide your name and the matter you wish to speak about. If you wish to speak to a particular
item on the current board agenda, your comments will be taken up at the time the Board takes up that item.
