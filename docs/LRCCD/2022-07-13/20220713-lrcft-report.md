LRCFT report to LRCCD Board of Trustees                        July 13, 2022


LRCFT negotiators continue to work with LRCCD to finalize our new mandatory
equity training component of our contract to be in place by the start of fall
semester.
LRCFT continues to meet with state leadership and local legislators to improve PT
faculty access to health care, job security, and pay parity.
The California Federation of Teachers and the LRCFT continue to lobby legislators
against passage of AB 1705.
The LRCFT is gearing back up to assist our local candidates endorsed by the LRCFT
for the November election.
The LRCFT is sponsoring three of our board members to attend the Los Rios trip to
Ghana in September.
Eight LRCFT exec. board members will also attend our national American
Federation of Teachers conference in Boston this week.
In closing, the LRCFT is proud to support the upcoming Naturalization and DACA
workshop being conducted by the Coalition for Humane Immigrant Rights
(CHIRLA). This in person event will be on Saturday, August 6th at Sacramento City
College starting at 9am. CHIRLA will provide free legal consultations, workshops
on Naturalization for those students and faculty and classified staff who are
permanent residents and wish to become US Citizens, as well as those who wish
to renew their DACA forms. CHIRLA will help with the costs for both applications
for qualifying individuals. We will offer more specifics shortly as to time and
location on the SCC Campus.
