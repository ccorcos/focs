                       Board of Trustees Report – July 2022
American River College Board of Trustees report
       Jessica Pressley, Classified Senate President
       The Classified Senate welcomes 6 new senators as we move into the new school year.
       Classified Senate will start to plan an on ground/virtual retreat for all new Officers and
       Senator’s.

Folsom Lake College

       Karla Lozano, Classified Senate President

       In coordination with the FLC Vice President of Administration and the Classified
       Professional Development Committee, Classified Senate is planning an on-ground New
       Employee Orientation and Falcon Buddy connection to take place this summer. The
       Falcon Buddy system pairs up Classified with new employees for three months to offer
       encouragement, guidance and to build connections. The FLC Caring Campus Team is
       planning to launch the program in August as well as present a workshop during Flex.

Cosumnes River College
       Tiffany Clark, Classified Senate President
       CRC Senate held an open forum meeting in June. VPA Theresa Tena gave an overview of
       the budget and the senate team who attended the Classified Leadership conference
       reported out on their experience. Senate meetings will resume in August.


Sacramento City College
       Kevin Williams, Classified Senate President.
       Sacramento City College has installed its new officers for the 22-23 year and is looking
       forward to a planning retreat to be held virtually. SCC officers attended the Classified
       Leadership Institute, and plans to implement ideas to help revitalize campus
       participation.
