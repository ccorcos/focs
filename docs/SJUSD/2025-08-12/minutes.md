Williams Complaint Report (I-10)
General Counsel Fhanysha Clark Gaddis explained that the district must report on the status of Williams-type complaints
filed with the district per Education Code section 35186(d), which requires each school district to publicly report, on a
quarterly basis, summarized data on the nature and resolution of all Williams-type complaints. Ms. Gaddis stated that,
during the time period from April 1, 2025, through June 30, 2025, there was one Williams-type complaint filed, which was
resolved.
