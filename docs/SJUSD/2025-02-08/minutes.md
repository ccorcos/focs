Effective Governance: Good Beginnings Workshop (B-1)
Mr. Avey called for public comment.

Public Comment:
Dave Warden expressed appreciation for board members stepping up into these positions.

Board members and Superintendent Bassanelli took part in the Good Beginnings Workshop facilitated by California School
Boards Association (CSBA) Governance Educator Luan Burman Rivera. The workshop was based on CSBA’s four building
blocks of effective governance: unity of purpose, roles and responsibilities, positive governance team culture (norms), and
supportive structures and processes (protocols).
