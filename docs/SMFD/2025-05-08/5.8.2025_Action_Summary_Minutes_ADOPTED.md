          Recommendation: Adopt Resolution designating the vehicles and equipment listed as
          surplus.
          Action: Adopted Resolution 2025-022.

          Maintenance Services

          Inc., and authorize the Fire Chief or his designee to negotiate, execute, and administer an
          agreement materially similar to the attachment agreement.
          Action: Adopted Resolution 2025-023.
