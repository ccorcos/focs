•    Alternative recommendation 1: Charge residential customers for power factor
     to reduce cost shifting, with a power factor charge requiring better metering and
     leading to more equitable rates.
        •      Response: This rate would be a significant change from current rates, would require
               significant investment, and would be extremely complex for customers to understand.

     better material resource planning by replacing its resource planning system, and
     create a standing committee for process improvement to receive a Deming
     Prize Award.
        •      Response: This recommendation is not relevant to the 2025 CEO & GM Report.
