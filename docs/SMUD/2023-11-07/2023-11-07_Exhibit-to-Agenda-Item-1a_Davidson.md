Commodity Highlights
Commodity budget funds the cost to provide energy to customers. It is
increasing by $87 million, or 17% to $572 million
• Continuing issues affecting the commodity budget
    Weather uncertainty
    Increased price volatility for both power and gas
    Increased costs in California’s carbon market
• New impacts to the commodity budget
    Significant cost increase for resource adequacy

Increasing mandates and growing competition for renewables and zero carbon
resources
• California mandates: achieve 44% Renewable Portfolio Standard (RPS) by 2024 and 52%
  by 2027
• Other western states, including Oregon and Washington are also adopting energy goals
