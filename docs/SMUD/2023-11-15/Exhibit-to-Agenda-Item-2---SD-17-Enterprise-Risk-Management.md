SD-17 Enterprise Risk Management (ERM)
Effectively balancing and managing risk to further SMUD’s policies
and business goals is a core value of SMUD.
Therefore:
SMUD will implement and maintain an integrated enterprise risk
management process that identifies, assesses, prudently manages
and mitigates a variety of risks facing SMUD, including financial,
supply, operational, physical and cyber security, climate change,
legal, legislative and regulatory, and reputational risk.

SMUD is compliant with SD-17, Enterprise Risk Management Policy
