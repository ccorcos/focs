Sacramento Municipal Utility District Financing Authority
(SFA) Bonds Defeasance
• In 2006, Sacramento Municipal Utility District Financing Authority (SFA) bonds were issued
  to finance Cosumnes Power Plant. The bonds were refinanced in 2015.

• Bonds are now “callable” and can be paid off ahead of their final 2030 maturity.

• Last outstanding bonds tied to SMUD’s gas power plants, which allows SMUD to
  decarbonize its debt portfolio.

• Repaying the bonds will help provide the flexibility needed to retool and further decarbonize
  SMUD’s portfolio.

• SFA assets will be combined with SMUD assets, creating efficiencies and cost savings.

• Reduces future debt service by $13.8 million per year through 2030.
