Grant funds are available for organizations from the Federal
Emergency Management Agency (FEMA) and the California
Governor’s Office of Emergency Services (Cal OES),
including the following:

• Disaster Recovery Public Assistance (PA)
• Building Resistant Infrastructure and Communities (BRIC)
• Hazard Mitigation Grant Program (HMGP)

SMUD currently has pending and awarded grants with
FEMA/Cal OES.

FEMA & Cal OES Authorized Agents
• FEMA and Cal OES require organizations designate “Authorized
  Agents”

• “Authorized Agents” are individuals who are endorsed by the
  governing body of an organization to engage with FEMA and Cal
  OES for the purpose of obtaining and managing certain federal
  financial assistance grants

• “Authorized Agents” are appointed every three years (from the last
  date of approval, May 19, 2022)
