This is to notify you that the Finance & Audit Committee of the Board of Directors will
meet on Tuesday, November 14, 2023, scheduled to begin at 6:00 p.m. in the SMUD
Headquarters Building Auditorium at 6201 S Street, Sacramento, California. A
remote telephonic location is also available at 1428 Matheson Drive, South Lake Tahoe,
CA 96150. This Committee meeting is noticed as a joint meeting with the Board of
Directors for the purpose of compliance with the Brown Act. In order to preserve the
function of the Committee as advisory to the Board, members of the Board may attend
and participate in discussions, but no Board action will be taken. The Finance & Audit
Committee will review, discuss and provide the Committee’s recommendation on the
agenda items.

Written Public Comment:
Members of the public may provide written public comment on a specific agenda item or
on items not on the agenda (general public comment) by submitting comments via email
to PublicComment@smud.org or by mailing or bringing physical copies to the meeting.
Comments will not be read into the record but will be provided to the Board and placed
into the record of the meeting if received within two hours after the meeting ends.
