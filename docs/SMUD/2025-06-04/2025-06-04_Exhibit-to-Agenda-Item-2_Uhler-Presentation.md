      Better metering (Power Factor)
´üČ   Electricity becomes a product "in the stream of
    commerce" at the utility meter
´üČ   Fong v. Pacific Gas & Electric Co. (1988) set in
    to law where electricity becomes a product
´üČ   Charging only for kilowatts overlooks power
    factor (PF) losses, requiring cost shifting in rates
´üČ   SMUD is installing 200,000 Itron Gen5 Riva
    meters for residential customer owners that
    measure PF by early 2026
Improved efficiency (Loading Order)
´üČ   Loading order places efficiency above adding
    any new generation to the grid
´üČ   Rates that cost shift hide efficiency losses
´üČ   SMUD's low use rate design is unstable with
    large differences within the customer type
    Less cost shifting (Equitable Rates)
´üČ   Charging for power factor (PF) losses will
    reduce cost shifting
´üČ   PF of 1 (unity) is best
´üČ   Customers inductive loads cause PF losses
´üČ   Appliances such as refrigerators, washing
    machines, swimming pool pumps are some of
    the higher PF losses
´üČ   Cost for part of these PF losses are shifted to
    customers who chose appliances with PF that
    are closer to unity
    Better Material Resource Planning

´üČ   It should not cost $408 to find out how many

    power factor (PF)
´üČ   SMUD staff appear unable to answer questions
    from the public on customer types directly

    report
´üČ   Detailed customer type data should appear in
    GM report for each customer type [PUC

    World War II
´üČ   Back in 1989 Florida Power & Light became the
    first non-Japanese winner of the award
