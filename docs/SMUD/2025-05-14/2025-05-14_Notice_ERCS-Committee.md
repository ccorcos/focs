                                            DISCUSSION ITEM

1.       Discuss authorizing the Chief Executive Officer and General Manager to
         negotiate and execute the Dry Creek Energy Storage, LLC (Dry Creek) Power
         Purchase Agreement (PPA) for a 20-year term, with one optional five-year
