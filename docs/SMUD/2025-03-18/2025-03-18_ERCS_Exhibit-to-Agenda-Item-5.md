Background

•     This contract was originally awarded competitively to KUBRA in October 2013 to provide a
      bill payment and presentment platform for a ten-year period for a not-to-exceed amount of
      $16,679,210.
•     Contract Changes 1-8 included fee adjustments, term extensions, minor scope changes,
      and terms and conditions modifications.
•     This Contract Change No. 9 is requested, rather than issuing a new solicitation, to allow for
      stability and continuity of these critical services through two major technology transitions,
      which include:
         •       i) a major upgrade to SMUD’s Enterprise Resource Planning platform to SAP S4/HANA and
         •       ii) the roll-out of a new Customer Experience Digital Platform with Smart Energy Water (SEW)/iPaySmart
