GP-12: Board Compensation & Benefits

GP -12 Sets forth Board’s policy for Board member compensation and
benefits

                 • Based on MUD Act section 11908.1 “…annual adjustment of not
                   more than five percent for each calendar year following the operative
                   date of the last adjustment.”
                 • Ability to increase amount no greater than $349.00 (not to exceed 10
                   days of service per month)
                 • Prohibited from automatically adjusting compensation every year

     Current Compensation:
       Board last updated per day compensation in July 2023 to $317.00 per
       event and meeting attendance (max of $3,170 per calendar month)
