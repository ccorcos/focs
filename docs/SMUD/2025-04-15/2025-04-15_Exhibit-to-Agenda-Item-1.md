Safety
Transition to Dead-Front Network Protectors:
•    Live-front models have exposed energized
     parts, posing safety risks.
•    Dead-front models have inaccessible
     energized parts, improving personnel safety.


Benefits:
•    Reduced risk of electrical contact incidents.
     Enhanced safety for SMUD personnel
     working on network protectors.
•    Compliance with SMUD’s safety and
     operational standards.
