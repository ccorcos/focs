**Scope of Work**

- Supply of Utility Wood Poles per SMUD’s engineering specifications.
- Maintain and replenish reserve stock at their facility.
- 20 different pole type stock codes, 35 feet to 85 feet of various diameter.
- Wood is southern yellow pine or douglas fir treated with “DCOI” or copper naphthenate preservative.

**Procurement Strategy**

Request for Proposal (RFP) was issued April 2024 to award contracts on a best value basis, yielding 3 qualified Suppliers.

The RFP was evaluated with the following criteria:

- 10%, SEED Program
- 45%, Technical

  - 23%, Experience
  - 17%, Responsiveness
- 45%, Commercial proposal
