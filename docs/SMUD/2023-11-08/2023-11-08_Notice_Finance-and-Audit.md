This is to notify you that the Finance & Audit Committee of the Board of Directors will
meet on Wednesday, November 8, 2023, scheduled to begin at 6:00 p.m. in the
SMUD Headquarters Building Auditorium at 6201 S Street, Sacramento, California.
This Committee meeting is noticed as a joint meeting with the Board of Directors for the
purpose of compliance with the Brown Act. In order to preserve the function of the
Committee as advisory to the Board, members of the Board may attend and participate
in discussions, but no Board action will be taken. The Finance & Audit Committee will
review, discuss and provide the Committee’s recommendation on the agenda items.
