1. Discussion and possible action on an agreement with MC Engineering related to FOWD Field Services Center Project  
   - General Manager Gray provided an overview of the proposal from MC Engineering, Inc. as requested by the Board for the Field Services Center Project.  
   - President Sarkovich provided an overview of the February 5, 2025, meeting at MC Engineering, Inc. that included Director Sarkovich, Director Petersen and General Manager Gray about the project. He stated that the Board does not have to make a decision about the proposal today. The consensus at the meeting is that there are three phases: relocation, demolition, and construction. He stated that FOWD should plan ahead for each phase and perhaps create separate contracts for each one.

**VI. DISCUSSION AND ACTION ITEMS: NEW BUSINESS**

1. **Discussion on FOWD Water Supply for the month of January 2025**
   - Water Supply Superintendent Siebensohn provided a summary of the water supply report.
   - General Manager Gray stated that water sales were up 23% in the first 6 months of the fiscal year for SJWD, while net revenues were up 10%. He highlighted this because it is a result of their rate structure.
   - SJWD Director McRae stated he is hopeful that a resolution will be reached that is happy for all parties involved.
   - General Manager Gray is equally hopeful that with the change in leadership starting at SJWD, some of the angst between FOWD and SJWD will reduce and that we can operate for mutual benefit in the near future or within a few years.

2. **Discussion on FOWD owned property located in Phoenix Park**
   - General Manager Gray provided a brief summary of how staff discovered FOWD owns a parcel within Phoenix Park for a well site.
   - General Manager Gray stated that staff recommends the Board direct the General Manager to schedule a meeting with the Fair Oaks Recreation and Park District (FORPD) to include Director Sarkovich, General Manager Gray, FORPD.
