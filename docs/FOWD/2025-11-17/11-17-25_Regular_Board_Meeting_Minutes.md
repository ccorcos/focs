V.    DISCUSSION AND ACTION ITEMS: OLD BUSINESS
      1. Update and discussion on the FOWD Northridge Well Project (verbal – no
         written staff report)
         • General Manager Gray provided an overview and update. He reported that well
            testing is complete and is expected to produce about 1,500 gallons.
         • President Sarkovich asked if there was feedback from RWA on the invoices
            submitted by FOWD.
         • General Manager Gray stated that the only feedback was a request for additional
            CEQA on the transmission main project.

      2. Update and discussion on the FOWD Transmission Main Replacement Project –
         Phase I (verbal – no written staff report)
         • General Manager Gray provided an overview and update. He reported that the
            paving issues with the County have been resolved at a savings of $170,000 for
            FOWD.

      3. Update and discussion on the New York Well Equipping Project (verbal – no
         written staff report)
         • General Manager Gray stated the contract for equipping is ready to be executed.
            He said that construction is scheduled to start in January.

      4. Discussion on FOWD Ten Year Summary of Financial Results
         • General Manager Gray provided an overview and update. He stated that operating
            expenses in the past 10 years have increased 67.4% and FOWD rate increases
            approved by the Board have increased by 35%. Over 40% of revenues from water
            sales have been used for capital improvements.
         • President Sarkovich stated that FOWD cannot count on outside grants for revenue
            and has to be cognizant of the increasing operating expenses.
