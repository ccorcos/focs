V.    DISCUSSION AND ACTION ITEMS: OLD BUSINESS
      1. Update and discussion on the Northridge Well Project (verbal – no written staff
         report)
         • General Manager Gray provided an overview and update. He reported that drilling
            is scheduled to be completed by October 15th. The well screening is being
            manufactured, it should only take a couple of days. After development of the well,
            he’ll be able to share an estimate of what the well production will be.

      2. Update and discussion on the FOWD Transmission Main Replacement Project –
         Phase I (verbal – no written staff report)
         • General Manager Gray provided an overview and update of the project. The
            pipeline is scheduled to be completed and back in service around September 18th.
            Paving will still need to be completed.
         • President Petersen inquired when the paving will be taking place.
         • Engineer Chetcuti responded it should take place around September/October.
         • General Manager Gray explained that FOWD is trying to have a one-time
            disruption. This is the reason for scheduling the paving right afterwards, it will
            avoid delays and further disruption to the neighborhood.
         • President Petersen inquired if everything is testing out okay with the new pipeline
            and inquired when the testing will be completed.
         • Engineer Chetcuti responded that the pressure test has been completed. The
            samples came back clean. Only three tie-ins remain.
         • General Manager Gray noted that all the inspection was done in-house. The
            Engineering Team and Construction Inspector Lahr performed the inspection,
            without the need to hire a third party.

      4. Update and discussion on the New York Well Equipping Project - Request for
         Bids (verbal – no written staff report)
         • General Manager Gray provided an overview and update. The well has been drilled

            for Proposal.

            pre-bid meeting a couple of weeks ago and there were about eight to ten different
            contractors present. There has been a good response and a lot of interest.
